ISSUE #1: CRITICAL QUOTATION ERROR IN SECTION 2.4 (A101.4)
Priority: HIGH
Status: REQUIRES IMMEDIATE CORRECTION
Location: Lines 204-206

Description:
The section titled "PROTECTION OF EMPLOYMENT (IBC 2024, Section A101.4)" contains
a direct quotation that does not accurately reflect the IBC 2024 A101.4 text.
Specifically, the quoted text includes "plans examiner" as a protected position,
but the actual IBC A101.4 only covers "building official, chief inspector or
inspector" - NOT plans examiners.

Why This Matters:
Section A101.3 covers "Inspector and plans examiner" qualifications, but the
protection of tenure in A101.4 applies only to inspectors, not plans examiners.
By including plans examiners in the A101.4 quotation, the officer file creates
an incorrect legal interpretation that could be problematic for Pakistan's
implementation.

Recommended Correction:
Replace the quoted text with the accurate IBC 2024 A101.4 wording:

"Employees in the position of building official, chief inspector or inspector
shall not be removed from office except for cause after full opportunity has been
given to be heard on specific charges before such applicable governing authority."

OR, if paraphrasing is preferred, mark it as such:

[A101.4 SUMMARY]: The building official, chief inspector, and inspector positions
are protected from removal except for cause, with full opportunity to be heard on
specific charges before the governing authority.

Note: Include clarification that plans examiners are NOT covered under this tenure
protection provision.

Description:

Section A101.2)" with specific responsibilities listed. However, the actual IBC
A101.2 uses the term "supervisors" rather than "chief inspector" explicitly, and
does not list these specific responsibilities. The officer file has reasonably
extrapolated the role, but this should be made explicit.

Additionally, IBC A101.2 specifies the scope includes International Mechanical Code

which is not mentioned in the officer file.

Recommended Correction:
Add a footnote or explanatory note to section 2.2:

by the building official. The listed responsibilities reflect the supervisory duties

International Mechanical Code (IMC), International Plumbing Code (IPC), and
International Fuel Gas Code (IFGC) provisions."
