SECTION 1: ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE
================================================================================

1.1 DEPARTMENT HIERARCHY (BNR 1994 Section A-B)

A. CHIEF ENGINEER (Paragraphs 1.38-1.41)
   - Apex authority for all B&R operations
   - Departmental policy formulation
   - Administrative sanction powers
   - Technical recommendation authority
   - Annual reporting to government
   - Budget allocation and resource management

B. REGIONAL/PROVINCIAL ENGINEER OFFICER (Paragraphs 1.42-1.43)
   - Provincial authority under Chief Engineer
   - Oversees multiple divisions
   - Regional project coordination
   - Intermediate approval authority

C. SUPERINTENDING ENGINEER (Paragraphs 1.44-1.51)
   - Divisional technical authority
   - Design review and approval
   - Budget management within delegation
   - Quality assurance oversight
   - Supervises 2-4 subdivisions

D. SUPERINTENDENT OF WORKS (Paragraph 1.52)
   - Field operational authority
   - Site supervision and inspection
   - Quality control verification
   - Progress monitoring

E. DIVISIONAL OFFICER/EXECUTIVE ENGINEER (Paragraphs 1.53-1.69)
   - Subdivision administrative head
   - Works coordination
   - Revenue collection
   - Personnel management
   - Public liaison

F. SUB-DIVISIONAL OFFICER (Paragraph 1.70)
   - Local implementation authority
   - Direct public interface
   - Minor works execution
   - Community coordination

SECTION 6: LEAVE PROVISIONS
================================================================================

6.1 LEAVE TYPES (BNR 1994 Section G)

A. EARNED LEAVE/ANNUAL LEAVE
   - Accrual: 2.5 days per month (30 days per year)
   - Maximum accumulation: 300 days
   - Encashment on retirement

B. CASUAL LEAVE
   - 15 days per calendar year
   - Non-accumulative
   - No prior sanction required (â‰¤3 days)

C. MEDICAL LEAVE
   - As certified by authorized medical officer
   - Half pay after exhaustion of earned leave
   - Medical certificate mandatory (>5 days)

D. EXTRAORDINARY LEAVE
   - Without pay
   - Granted in exceptional circumstances
   - Does not count toward service

E. MATERNITY/PATERNITY LEAVE
   - Maternity: 180 days (Pakistan law)
   - Paternity: 15 days
   - Adoption leave: 90 days

6.2 LEAVE SANCTIONING AUTHORITY
   - Sub-Divisional Officer: Up to 30 days
   - Divisional Officer: Up to 60 days
   - Superintending Engineer: Up to 120 days
   - Chief Engineer: Beyond 120 days
