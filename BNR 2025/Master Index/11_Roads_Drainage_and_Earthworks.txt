KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS (KPIs) FOR ROADS DRAINAGE
================================================================================

Hydraulic Performance
    - Peak flow reduction percentage (%)
    - Attenuation efficiency
    - Runoff volume reduction (%)
    - Water quality improvements (pollutant removal %)
    - Detention time and residence time
    - Infiltration rates (mm/hr)

Structural Performance
    - Embankment settlement (mm)
    - Pavement surface regularity (IRI, texture depth)
    - Drainage system capacity vs. design storm
    - Slope stability factor of safety
    - Compaction percentage of optimum

Environmental Performance
    - Biodiversity index (species count, habitat diversity)
    - Air quality improvement (PM2.5, NOx reduction)
    - Noise reduction (dB)
    - Carbon footprint (kg CO2e)
    - Embodied carbon in materials
    - Water quality parameters (turbidity, oils, nutrients)

Economic Performance
    - Cost per unit area of drainage
    - Maintenance cost per year
    - Cost avoidance from flood prevention
    - Whole-life cost analysis
    - Cost per hectare of habitat created

Social Performance
    - Public accessibility to green space
    - Community engagement and satisfaction
    - Landscape amenity value
    - Safety incident rate (construction phase)
    - Accessibility for people with disabilities ⭐
