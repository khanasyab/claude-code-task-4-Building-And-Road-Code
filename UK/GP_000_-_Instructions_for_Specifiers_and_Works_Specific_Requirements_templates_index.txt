Foreword
This index lists the Instructions for Specifiers documents and Works Specific Requirements documents
that enable the Specifier to compile the Works Specific Requirements, that together with the
Specification for Highways Works, supplementary and alternative clauses, form the contract
specification.
Some documents may have country specific requirements. Users are responsible for applying all
appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.
3
