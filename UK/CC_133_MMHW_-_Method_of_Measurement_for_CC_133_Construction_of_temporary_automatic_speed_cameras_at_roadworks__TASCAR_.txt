1. Temporary automatic speed cameras at roadwork...
1.
Temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks (TASCAR)
Units
1.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
installation of temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks ............ item.
b)
maintenance of temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks ............ day.
c)
removal of temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks ............ item.
1.2
The measurement of installation and removal of temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks
shall be for the complete installation and removal and measured once only.
1.3
The measurement of maintenance of temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks shall be each
day or part thereof during which the system is provided.
Itemisation
1.4
Separate items shall be provided for temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks in accordance
with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 4.N] and the following:
Table 1.4 Itemisation forTemporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks
Group
Feature
I
1 Installation of temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks.
2 Maintenance of temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks.
3 Removal of temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks.
II
1 Different locations.
III
1 Different lengths.
Item Coverage - Installation of temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks
1.5
The items for installation of temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks shall be in accordance
with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 3.N] and include for:
a)
excavation of acceptable earthworks material as clauses 3.4 and 3.5 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 6.N];
b)
excavation of unacceptable earthworks material as clause 3.6 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 6.N];
c)
excavation of Hard Material as clause 4.4 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 6.N];
d)
concrete as clauses 1.4 and 2.5 of CC 482/MMHW [Ref 5.N];
e)
steel reinforcement as clause 5.8 of CC 482/MMHW [Ref 5.N];
f)
backfilling and compaction;
g)
formwork as clause 3.9 of CC 482/MMHW [Ref 5.N];
h)
disposal of unacceptable earthworks material as clause 7.6 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 6.N];
i)
electrical equipment, wiring and connections;
j)
cameras and associated equipment;
k)
housings, camera poles and supports, associated safety fencing, paving and handrails;
l)
control and monitoring equipment;
m)
replacement equipment;
n)
complying with wiring regulations, earthing and inspection;
o)
earth electrodes as clause 8.4 of TC 501/MMHW [Ref 8.N];

Item Coverage - Maintenance of temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks
1.6
The items for maintenance of temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks shall be in accordance
with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 3.N] and include for:
a)
depreciation, maintenance and repair of equipment;
b)
electrical power;
c)
wiring protection;
d)
modifications including moving equipment to suit traffic management and phasing of the works;
e)
liaison with the EA and providing access for them at all times;
f)
assistance requested by the EA through the Overseeing Organisation;
g)
provision of evidential storage media.
Item Coverage - Removal of temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks
1.7
The items for removal of temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks shall be in accordance with
clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 3.N] and include for:
a)
dismantling;
b)
decommissioning;
c)
reinstatement and making good;
d)
take up or down and set aside for reuse or remove to store or tip off site as clause 4.5 of
GC 109/MMHW [Ref 7.N].
Item Coverage - Construction of temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks (TASCAR)
designed by the Contractor
1.8
The respective coverage rules for temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks above shall be
replaced and/or supplemented with the items below where temporary automatic speed cameras at
roadworks, or aspects thereof, are to be Contractor designed.
1.9
The items for temporary automatic speed cameras at roadworks, or aspects thereof designed by the
Contractor, shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 3.N] and include for:
a)
design in accordance with CD 133 [Ref 1.N] and Section 17 of GC 101 [Ref 2.N];
b)
provision of design calculations and details to the Overseeing Organisation for acceptance;
c)
reports and certificates;
d)
provision of data and drawings;
e)
re-submissions and modifications;
