Foreword

Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1. Sweeping and cleaning, litter picking, applic...
1.
Sweeping and cleaning, litter picking, application of herbicide,
removal of graffiti and posters, cleaning and emptying of litter
bins and disposal of waste
Units
1.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
sweeping and cleaning, and litter picking ............ kilometre, square metre or hour.
b)
application of herbicide, and removal of graffiti and posters ............ square metre.
c)
cleaning and emptying of litter and waste bins ............ number.
d)
disposal of waste ............ tonne.
Measurement
1.2
The measurement (kilometre) of sweeping and cleaning shall be the linear length of associated
highway.
1.3
The measurement (kilometre) of litter picking shall be the linear length of associated highway from
verge to boundary irrespective of the width of clearance.
1.4
The measurement (square metres) of sweeping and cleaning, and litter picking shall be the plan area.
1.5
The measurement (hour) of sweeping and cleaning, and litter picking shall be each hour during which
the Contractor is required to provide the service as agreed with the Overseeing Organisation and
subject to a minimum measurement of two hours.
1.6
The measurement (hour) of part hours for sweeping and cleaning, and litter picking shall be rounded up
to the nearest hour.
1.7
The measurement of application of herbicide and removal of graffiti and posters shall be the plan or
elevation area subject to a minimum measurement of five square metres.
1.8
The measurement of cleaning and emptying of litter and waste bins shall be each litter and waste bin.
1.9
The measurement of disposal of waste by tonne shall be calculated from the tonnage of waste disposal
certified by the Overseeing Organisation.
1.10
The tonnage certified by the Overseeing Organisation shall be only that waste included on waste
disposal tickets which has arisen from the task.
1.11
Waste in excess of the requirements of the task and waste arising from any other purpose shall not be
included within the certified tonnage.
Itemisation
1.12
Separate items shall be provided for sweeping and cleaning, litter picking, application of herbicide,
removal of graffiti and posters, cleaning and emptying of litter and waste bins, and disposal of waste in
accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 2.N] and the following:

Item Coverage - Sweeping and cleaning, and disposal of waste
1.13
The items for sweeping and cleaning, and disposal of waste shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of
GP 113 [Ref 1.N] and include for:
a)
compliance with all legislation not limited to legislation affecting use of vehicles on the public
highway and the like;
b)
equipment inspections;
c)
bringing equipment to site;
d)
maintenance of equipment including communication equipment in accordance with
manufacturer's recommendations and identification signs;
e)
comprehensive insurance;
f)
replacement equipment;
g)
depreciation;
h)
maintenance;
i)
fuel, oil and other consumables;
j)
qualified operatives and technicians;
k)
liaising with police;
l)
vehicle inspections;
