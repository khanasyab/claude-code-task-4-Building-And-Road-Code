Foreword
Foreword
This document provides specifier instructions for the production of the works specific requirements for
GC 104 Works data, third party aspects and customer communications.
This document does not form part of the works specification.
The works specification is made up of both the Specification for Highway Works and the works specific
requirements completed by the Specifier.
This document is applicable for contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional
specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

Existing ground levels
1.21
Verification shall be undertaken for existing ground levels to check them against the setting out data
and information detailed in paragraph 1.1 of this Section.
1.22
The frequency of the check on existing ground levels shall be once, prior to the commencement of work
to the ground being checked.
1.23
The requirements for "Verification" in Section 14 of GC 101 [Ref 6.N] shall apply to the check on
existing ground levels.
1.24
The following Documentation shall be submitted for the check on existing ground levels prior to the
commencement of work to the ground being checked: records of the check and levels for areas where
there is discrepancy with the setting out data and information.
1.25
The requirements for "Documentation" in Section 2 of GC 101 [Ref 6.N] shall apply to records of the
check on existing ground levels and levels for areas where there is discrepancy with the setting out
data and information.

2. Utility services and supplies
2.
Utility services and supplies
2.1
For the purposes of the specification 'utility services and supplies' shall include all statutory
undertakers' services and supplies, other publicly or privately owned services and supplies and all
associated apparatus, including those for highway purposes.
E/2.2
Utility services shall include the National Roads Telecommunications Services.
2.3
The position of utility services and supplies which are affected, or possibly affected, by the works shall
be established.
2.4
Utility services and supplies shall not be damaged by the works.
2.5
Throughout the progress of the works measures shall be taken to support and fully protect utility
services and supplies which are affected, or possibly affected, by the works.
2.6
Utility services and supplies shall not be interrupted without prior arrangement with the utility service or
supply company, owner or operating agent.
2.7
Utility services and supplies to individual properties shall not be interrupted without liaison with the
owners or occupiers of the individual property.
2.8
Works to roadside technology and communications shall be undertaken in accordance with TC 131
[Ref 10.N] Roadside technology and communications.
2.9
The known details and locations of utility services and supplies shall be as stated in GC 104/WSR/002.
Works Specific Requirements to be provided within the WSR template where applicable are:
SI.2.9
Details and locations of known utility services and supplies within, or near to, the site shall be as shown
on the following drawing(s)/model(s) [enter free text].
Diversionary works to utility services and supplies
2.10
Where utility services or supplies are subject to diversionary works liaison shall be undertaken with the
utility service or supply company, owner or operating agent.
2.11
Where utility services or supplies are subject to diversionary works the liaison with the utility service or
supply owner or operating agent shall include establishing arrangements and programming for
undertaking the diversionary works.
2.12
Diversionary works for utility services or supplies to be undertaken by third parties, that require to be
programmed for and liaison for shall be as detailed in GC 104/WSR/002.
Works Specific Requirements to be provided within the WSR template where applicable are:
