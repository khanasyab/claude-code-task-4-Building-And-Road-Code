Note
Hydrophobic impregnation of structural concrete shall be measured under CC 488/MMHW [Ref 7.N].
Units
1.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
structural concrete ............ cubic metre.
Measurement
1.2

a)

3. Falsework and formwork for structural concret...
3.
Falsework and formwork for structural concrete
Units
3.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
formwork ............ square metre.
b)
void formers ............ linear metre.
Measurement
3.2
The measurement shall be the area of formwork which is in contact with the finished structural concrete
but measured over the face of openings of 1 square metre or less and features described in clause 3.3
c) of this document below.
3.3
Formwork shall not be measured to:
a)
construction joints whether or not shown on the Drawings;
b)
holes, ducts, pockets, sockets, mortices and the like, not exceeding 0.15 cubic metres each in
volume;
c)
individual fillets, chamfers, splays, drips, rebates, recesses, grooves and the like of 100 mm total
girth or less when measured overall the faces in contact with the structural concrete;
d)
edges of blinding concrete 75 mm or less in thickness;
e)
upper surfaces of structural concrete inclined at an angle of less than 15o to the horizontal;
f)
unformed surfaces.
3.4
Where structural concrete, other than blinding concrete 75 mm or less in thickness, is placed in
structural foundations, formwork shall be measured to the sides of such structural concrete foundations
regardless of whether or not any formwork is used, except where it is expressly stated on the Drawings
that the structural concrete is to be cast against the soil face.
3.5
The following shall apply for measurement of formwork:
a)
"horizontal" shall include formwork horizontal or inclined at any angle up to and including 5o to the
horizontal.
b)
"inclined" shall include formwork inclined at any angle more than 5o up to and including 85o to the
horizontal.
c)
"vertical" shall include formwork inclined at any angle more than 85o up to and including 90o to
the horizontal.
d)
"at any inclination" shall include formwork horizontal or inclined at any angle up to and including
90o to the horizontal.
3.6
The measurement of void formers shall be the length measured along the centre line of the void former,
and measured whether of a permanent or temporary nature.
3.7
Formwork curved or hogged in construction before the placement of structural concrete and designed
to achieve a permanent flat finish shall not be measured as curved.
Note a
Formwork required to produce curved falls and cambers is measured as curved formwork.
Note b
Unformed finishes (U1 to U5 etc.) should not be measured and are covered by the item coverage in
clause 1.3 of this document.
Itemisation
3.8
Separate items shall be provided for formwork in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112
[Ref 3.N] and the following:

4. Falsework and patterned profile formwork for ...
4.
Falsework and patterned profile formwork for structural concrete
Definition
4.1
The term "patterned profile formwork" shall be formwork designed to produce a structural concrete face
with a specified patterned profile comprising ribs, corrugations, troughs or other patterns in relief.
4.2
Formwork with a specified regular pattern of formwork joints shall not be classified as patterned profile
formwork.
Units
4.3
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
patterned profile formwork ............ square metre.
Measurement
4.4
The measurement shall be the flat undeveloped area of the patterned structural concrete required by
the Contract and measured over the face of openings of 1 square metre or less and features described
in clause 4.5 c) of this document below.
4.5
Patterned profile formwork shall not be measured to:
a)
construction joints whether or not shown on the Drawings;
b)
holes, ducts, pockets, sockets, mortices and the like, not exceeding 0.15 cubic metres each in
volume;
c)
individual fillets, chamfers, splays, drips, rebates, recesses, grooves and the like, not forming part
of the pattern and of 100 mm total girth or less when measured overall the faces in contact with
the structural concrete;
d)
edge of blinding concrete 75 mm or less in thickness;
e)
upper surfaces of structural concrete inclined at an angle of less than 15o to the horizontal.
4.6
The following shall apply for measurement of patterned profile formwork:
a)
"horizontal" shall include patterned profile formwork horizontal or inclined at any angle up to and
including 5o to the horizontal.
b)
"inclined" shall include patterned profile formwork inclined at any angle more than 5o up to and
including 85o to the horizontal.
c)
"vertical" shall include patterned profile formwork inclined at any angle more than 85o up to and
including 90o to the horizontal.
d)
"at any inclination" shall include patterned profile formwork horizontal or inclined at any angle up
to and including 90o to the horizontal.
Itemisation

Units

The units of measurement shall be:
a)
bar and helical reinforcement ............ tonne.
b)

Measurement
5.2
The mass of plain bar reinforcement shall be calculated on the basis that the nominal density of steel is

5.3

7. Prestressing of structural concrete
7.
Prestressing of structural concrete
Note
This measurement section pertains to pre-tensioning or post tensioning of in situ structural concrete.
Any pre-tensioning or post tensioning associated with the manufacture of precast concrete elements
whether on site or off site, is item coverage under clause 2.5 of this document .
Units
7.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
pre-tensioned and post-tensioned tendons ............ number (stating the length) or linear metre.
b)
pre-tensioning and post-tensioning of structural concrete ............ number (stating the length) or
linear metre.
Note
For the purpose of CC 482/MMHW [Ref 5.N] a tendon is defined as all the permanent components of a
system which imparts a compressive load to a concrete member through a single anchorage or bearing
plate at each end of the system.
Measurement
7.2
Lengths of tendons shall be measured along the line of the tendon between the outside faces of those
parts of the anchorage units cast into the concrete.
7.3
Tendons shall be grouped so that no member of the group differs in length from the stated length by
more than 5%.
Itemisation
7.4
Separate items shall be provided for pre-tensioned and post-tensioned tendons in accordance with
clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:

Units
8.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
vertical rods, strip and bar reinforcing elements ............ linear metre.

sheet reinforcing elements ............ square metre.
Measurement
8.2
The measurement of vertical rods shall be the length from the top surface of the strip footing to the top
of the facing unit or the top of the rod whichever is the higher.
8.3
The measurement of strip and bar reinforcing elements shall be the overall length including
connections and, where applicable, the turn down for end anchorages.
8.4
Where a strip element comprises more than one leg, measurement shall be of all legs, with the number
of legs measured being stated in the item description.
8.5
Measurement of sheet reinforcing elements shall be the summation of the areas of each layer.
