Foreword
Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1. Fencing, gates and stiles
1.
Fencing, gates and stiles
Units
1.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
fencing ............ linear metre.
b)
concrete foundation to timber posts ............ number.
c)
gates, stiles ............ number.
d)
fenced tree guards ............ number.
Measurement
1.2
Where a particular type of temporary fencing is specified in LC 127 [Ref 1.N] by the Overseeing
Organisation and shown on the drawings it shall be measured.
1.3
All other types of temporary fencing shall not be measured.
1.4
The measurement of fencing shall be the developed length along the centre line of the fence.
1.5
The measurement of height of fencing shall be that stated in the Contract for the type of fence.
1.6
The measurement of width of gates shall be the distance between the outer edges of the stiles; the
outer edges of hanging stiles in the case of double gates.
1.7
The measurement of height of gates shall be the distance between the upper surface of the top rail and
the underside of the bottom rail.
1.8
Concrete foundation to timber posts shall only be measured for those locations stated in LC 127 [Ref
1.N].
1.9
Foundations other than concrete foundations to timber posts shall be deemed to be included within the
fencing item to which they relate.
Itemisation
1.10
Separate items shall be provided for fencing in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112
[Ref 4.N] and the following:
Table 1.10 Itemisation for Fencing, gates and stiles
Group
Feature
I
1 Each type of fencing.
2 Concrete foundation to each type of timber post for each type of fencing.
3 Each type of gate.
4 Each type of stile.
5 Each type of fenced tree guard.
II
1 Each type of additional fencing component to fencing.
III
1 Fencing of different heights.
2 Gates of different heights and widths.
IV
1 Painted fencing, gates or stiles.

excavation of acceptable earthworks material as clauses 3.4 and 3.5 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 8.N];

trimming ground at entrance;
d)
structural concrete as clause 1.4 of CC 482/MMHW [Ref 6.N];
e)
formwork as clause 3.9 of CC 482/MMHW [Ref 6.N];

steel reinforcement as clause 5.8 of CC 482/MMHW [Ref 6.N];

o)

Item Coverage - Fencing, gates and stiles designed by the Contractor
1.14
The respective coverage rules for fencing, gates and stiles above shall be replaced and/or
supplemented with the items below where fencing, gates and stiles are to be designed by the
Contractor.
1.15
The item coverage for fencing, gates and stiles designed by the Contractor shall be in accordance with
clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 3.N] and include for:
a)
design in accordance with WSR/001 of LC 127 [Ref 1.N] , LD 117 [Ref 5.N] and Section 17 of GC
101 [Ref 2.N];
b)
reports and certificates;
c)
provision of data and drawings;
d)
re-submissions and modifications;
e)
amendments to the Works;
f)
obtaining approvals;
g)
everything necessary for the design, fabrication, installation and verification of the Works.

2. Repairs to fencing, gates and stiles
2.
Repairs to fencing, gates and stiles
Units
2.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
repairs to fencing ............ linear metre or item.
b)
repairs to gates and stiles ............ item.
Measurement
2.2
The measurement of repairs to fencing shall be either the developed length along the centre line of
fence (linear metres) or the complete repair (item).
2.3
The measurement of repairs to gates and stiles shall be the complete repair.
Itemisation
2.4
Separate items shall be provided for repairs to fencing, gates and stiles in accordance with clauses 2.2,
2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 4.N] and the following:
Table 2.4 Itemisation for Repairs to fencing, gates and stiles
Group
Feature
I
1 Repairs to each type of fencing.
2 Repairs to each type of gate.
3 Repairs to each type of stile.
II
1 Fencing of different heights.
2 Gates of different heights and widths.
III
1 Painted fencing, gates or stiles.
Item coverage - Repairs to fencing
2.5
The items for repairs to fencing shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 3.N] and include
for:
a)
fencing as clause 1.11 of this document.
Item Coverage - Repairs to gates and stiles
2.6
The items for repairs to gates and stiles shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 3.N] and
include for:
a)
gates and stiles as clause 1.13 of this document.

Units

concrete foundation to timber posts ............ number.

remove from store and re-erect gates and stiles ............ number.
Measurement
3.2
The measurement of re-erected fencing shall be the developed length along the centre line of the
re-erected fencing.

outer edges of hanging stiles in the case of double gates.

1.N].

4. Excavation in Hard Material
4.
Excavation in Hard Material
Units
4.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
extra over excavation for excavation in Hard Material in fencing works ............ cubic metre.
Measurement
4.2
The measurement of extra over excavation for excavation in Hard Material in fencing works shall be the
plan area of the minimum size of the particular foundation required by the Contract multiplied by the
depth of Hard Material removed.
Itemisation
4.3
Separate items shall be provided for extra over excavation for excavation in Hard Material in fencing
works in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 4.N] and the following:
Table 4.3 Itemisation for Excavation in Hard Material
Group
Feature
I
1 Extra over for excavation in Hard Material in fencing works.
Item Coverage - Extra over excavation for in excavation in Hard Material
4.4
The items for extra over excavation for excavation in Hard Material in fencing works shall be in
accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 3.N] and include for:
a)
excavation in Hard Material as clause 4.4 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 8.N].
