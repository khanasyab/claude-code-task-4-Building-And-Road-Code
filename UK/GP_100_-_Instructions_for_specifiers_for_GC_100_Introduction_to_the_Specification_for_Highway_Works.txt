Summary
This document gives instructions for specifiers with respect to the Introduction to the
Specification for Highway Works in order to define the specification for the contract, identify the
Overseeing Organisation, bring in the contract drawings or models and identify a contract
reference document date.
Feedback and Enquiries
Users of this document are encouraged to raise any enquiries and/or provide feedback on the content and usage of
this document to the dedicated National Highways team. The online feedback form for all enquiries and feedback
can be accessed at: www.standardsforhighways.co.uk/feedback.
This is a controlled document.

Foreword
Foreword
This document provides specifier instructions for the production of the works specific requirements for
GC 100 Introduction to the Specification for Highway Works.
This document does not form part of the works specification.
The works specification is made up of both the Specification for Highway Works and the works specific
requirements completed by the Specifier.
This document is applicable for contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional
specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1. The contract specification
1.
The contract specification
1.1
The specification referred to in the contract shall be the following:
1)
the Specification for Highway Works (SHW); and
2)
the Works Specific Requirements (WSR) for the contract listed in GC 100/WSR/001; and
3)
the Works Specific Requirements (WSR) that modify the Specification for Highway Works
identified in GC 100/WSR/001.
1.2
The national version of the Specification for Highway Works to be used for the contract shall be as
stated in GC 100/WSR/001.
Works Specific Requirements to be provided within the WSR template where applicable are:
SI.1.2
The national version of the Specification for Highway Works to be used for the contract shall be [select
one from: the version for England, the version for Northern Ireland, the version for Scotland, the
version for Wales].
1.3
The versions of SHW documents that are applicable to the specification shall be as stated in
GC 100/WSR/001.

All requirements and obligations stated in the specification shall be requirements and obligations of the
Contractor.
1.6

2. Overseeing Organisation
2.
Overseeing Organisation
2.1
The Overseeing Organisation shall be as stated in GC 100/WSR/002.
Works Specific Requirements to be provided within the WSR template where applicable are:
SI.2.1
The Overseeing Organisation of the contract shall be [select one from: the Department for
Infrastructure Northern Ireland, National Highways, Transport Scotland, Welsh Government].
2.2
The parties undertaking the roles and functions of the Overseeing Organisation shall be as stated in
GC 100/WSR/002.
Works Specific Requirements to be provided within the WSR template where applicable are:
SI.2.2

4. Specification reference documents
4.
Specification reference documents
4.1
Unless otherwise stated in the specification or directed by legislation, where standards and other
documents are referenced in the specification the respective edition used shall be that which is current
at the contract reference document date.
4.2
The contract reference document date shall be as stated in GC 100/WSR/004.
Works Specific Requirements to be provided within the WSR template where applicable are:
SI.4.2
The contract reference document date is [enter free text].
4.3
Where no date is stated in GC 100/WSR/004 the contract reference document date shall be the tender
invitation date.
