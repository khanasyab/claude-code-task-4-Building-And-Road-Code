Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1.
Protective systems for protection of steelwork against corrosion
Units
1.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
protective system to general surfaces, minor structures and the like ............ square metre.
b)
protective system to bridges, pedestrian restraint systems, parapets and the like ............ linear
metre.
c)
protective system to bridge bearings, metal coated fasteners and the like ............ number.
Measurement
1.2
The measurement of protective system to general surfaces, minor structures and the like shall be the
surface area to be treated.
1.3
The measurement of protective system to bridges, pedestrian restraint systems, parapets and the like
shall be the developed length (on plan) to be treated.
1.4
The measurement of protective system to bridge bearings, metal coated fasteners and the like shall be
the complete unit.
Itemisation
1.5
Separate items shall be provided for protection system for protection of steelwork against corrosion in
accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 2.N] and the following:
Table 1.5 Itemisation for Protective systems for protection of steelwork against corrosion
Group
Feature

II
1 Bridges.
2 Parapets.
3 Bridge bearings.
4 Metal coated fasteners.
5 Minor structures.
6 Pedestrian restraint systems.
7 Other highway structures.
III
1 Different types of corrosion protection system.
IV
1 Stated environment.
V
1 Different locations.
Item Coverage - Protective systems for protection of steelwork against corrosion
1.6
The items for protective system for protection of steelwork against corrosion shall be in accordance
with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 1.N] and include for:
a)
despatching paint samples to testing authority;
b)
masking and other measures to protect adjacent untreated work and the removal of masking and
other measures upon completion;
c)
joint fillers and treatment of joints;
d)
preparing materials for application;
e)
preparation of surfaces and coating at the place of fabrication and on Site;
