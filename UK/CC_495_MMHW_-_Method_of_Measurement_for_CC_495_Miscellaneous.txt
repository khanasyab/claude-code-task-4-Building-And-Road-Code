Foreword
Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1. Foamed concrete for structures
1.
Foamed concrete for structures
Units
1.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
foamed concrete for structures ............ item.
Measurement
1.2
The measurement of foamed concrete for structures shall be for the complete construction.
Itemisation
1.3
Separate items shall be provided for foamed concrete for structures in accordance with clauses 2.2,
2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 1.3 Itemisation for Foamed concrete for structures
Group
Feature
I
1 Foamed concrete infill to structure.
2 Foamed concrete infill to stated structural element.
3 Foamed concrete infill to other stated asset.
II
1 Different classes.
Item Coverage - Foamed concrete for structures
1.4
The items for foamed concrete for structures shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 2.N]
and include for:
a)
design in accordance with CD 350 [Ref 5.N] and Section 17 of GC 101 [Ref 1.N];
b)
reports and certificates;
c)
provision of data and drawings;
d)
re-submissions and modifications;
e)
amendments to the Works;
f)
obtaining approvals;
g)
everything necessary for the design, fabrication, installation and verification of the Works.

S/2. Cored node markers
S/2.
Cored node markers
Units
S/2.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
cored node markers ............ number.
Itemisation
S/2.2
Separate items shall be provided for cored node markers in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4
of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table S/2.2 Itemisation for Cored node markers
Group
Feature
I
1 Cored node markers.
II
1 Different types.
III
1 Different sizes.
Item coverage - Cored node markers
S/2.3
The items for cored node markers shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and
include for:
a)
coring pockets in carriageway;
b)
cleaning and drying pockets;
c)
disposal of unacceptable earthworks material as clause 7.6 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 4.N];
d)
incorporation of solid glass beads;
e)
referencing and recording location of completed node markers.
