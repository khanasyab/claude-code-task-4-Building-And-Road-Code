1. Temporary traffic signs
1.
Temporary traffic signs
Note
Temporary traffic signs, road markings and road studs etc. that form part of temporary traffic
management installations or closures are item coverage within GC 106/MMHW [Ref 6.N].
Units
1.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
temporary traffic signs ............ number.
Measurement
1.2
The measurement of temporary traffic signs shall be the complete installation except for earth
electrodes which are measured separately under clauses 8.1, 8.2, 8.2 Note, 8.3, and 8.4 of
TC 501/MMHW [Ref 9.N].
Itemisation
1.3
Separate items shall be provided for temporary traffic signs in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and
2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 4.N] and the following:
Table 1.3 Itemisation for Temporary traffic signs
Group
Feature
I
1 Temporary traffic signs.
II
1 Particular sign reference.
III
1 Retroreflective.
2 Non-retroreflective.
3 Enhanced retroreflective.
IV
1 Transilluminated.
2 Externally illuminated.
3 Non illuminated.
V
1 Different types.
VI
1 Different sizes.
VII
1 Different posts or supports.
Item Coverage - Temporary traffic signs
1.4
The items for temporary traffic signs shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 3.N] and
include for:
a)
permanent upright traffic signs as clause 1.4 of CC 120/MMHW [Ref 5.N];
b)
take up or down and set aside for reuse or remove to store off Site as clause 4.5 of
GC 109/MMHW [Ref 7.N];
c)
maintaining and servicing equipment;
d)
ballast to secure temporary signs from overturning or falling.

4. Temporary traffic signs and road markings des...
4.
Temporary traffic signs and road markings designed by the
Contractor
Item coverage - Temporary traffic signs designed by the Contractor
4.1
The respective coverage rules for temporary traffic signs and road markings above shall be replaced
and/or supplemented with the items below where temporary traffic signs and road markings, or
elements and components thereof, are to be designed by the Contractor.
4.2
The items for temporary traffic signs and road markings or elements and components thereof designed
by the Contractor, shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 3.N] and include for:
a)
design in accordance with CD 130 [Ref 1.N] and Section 17 of GC 101 [Ref 2.N];
b)
reports and certificates;
c)
provision of data and drawings;
d)
re-submissions and modifications;
e)
amendments to the Works;
f)
obtaining approvals;
g)
everything necessary for the design, fabrication, installation and verification of the Works.
