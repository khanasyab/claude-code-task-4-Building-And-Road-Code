1. Bridge deck expansion joints
1.
Bridge deck expansion joints
Definition
1.1
The term "bridge deck expansion joint" covers all types of permanent joint which shall allow expansion,
contraction, shrinkage or angular rotation to take place in decks of structures.
Units
1.2
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
bridge deck expansion joints ............ number, linear metre or cubic metre.
Measurement
1.3
The measurement of bridge deck expansion joints shall be the complete installation.
1.4
The stated length (number) shall be measured along the centre line of the joint.
1.5
The measurement of bridge deck expansion joints measured as linear metres and cubic metres shall
be measured along the centre line of the joint.
Itemisation
1.6
Separate items shall be provided for bridge deck expansion joints in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3,
and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 2.N]and the following:
Table 1.6 Itemisation for Bridge deck expansion joints
Group
Feature
I
1 Bridge deck expansion joints.
II
1 Different types or materials.
III
1 Different lengths.
IV
1 Different gap widths.
Note: Group III Feature I shall be applied only to stated lengths of bridge deck expansion joints.
Item Coverage - Bridge deck expansion joints
1.7
The items for bridge deck expansion joints shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 1.N]
and include for:
a)
preparing and cleaning surfaces;
b)
installing or constructing the joint, sub-surface drainage, below joint drainage, in situ nosings, and
waterproofing complete with fittings at kerbs, footways, service ducts and the like including the
use of templates, guides and the like to retain the joint system in position;
c)
setting the joint having regard to temperature and other constraints;
d)
priming surfaces to be sealed, joint filler material, sealing strips, inserting, protecting and sealing;
e)
adhesives and the like;
f)
forming, cutting and sealing grooves and edges in surfacings and bridge deck waterproofing;
g)
protective systems as clause 1.6 of CC 486/MMHW [Ref 3.N];
h)
greasing;
i)
measures to protect the joint against damage or displacement;
j)
drilling or forming holes and pockets and casting in bolts, sockets, base plates and anchorage
assemblies.

2. Sealing of gaps in bridges and structures
2.
Sealing of gaps in bridges and structures
Units
2.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
joint filler ............ square metre.
b)
joint sealant, water stop ............ linear metre.
Measurement
2.2
The measurement of joint filler shall be the area of the surface to be covered as stated in the Contract.
2.3
The measurement of joint sealant shall be the length of the joint on the external face of the sealant.
2.4
The measurement of water stop shall be the length along the axis.
2.5
Joint filler and joint sealant shall not be measured to bridge deck expansion joints.
Itemisation
2.6
Separate items shall be provided for sealing of gaps in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of
GP 112 [Ref 2.N] and the following:
Table 2.6 Itemisation for Sealing of gaps in bridges and structures
Group
Feature
I
1 Joint filler.
2 Joint sealant.
3 Water stop.
II
1 Different types or materials.
III
1 Different sizes.
IV
1 Different thicknesses.
Item Coverage - Joint filler
2.7
The items for joint filler shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 1.N] and include for:
a)
cutting and shaping;
b)
preparing, cleaning and priming surfaces;
c)
adhesives and the like;
d)
applying, inserting and casting in.
Item Coverage - Joint sealant
2.8
The items for joint sealant shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 1.N] and include for:
a)
preparing and cleaning surfaces;
b)
priming the surface of the joint;
c)
compressible strip;
d)
masking and protection;
e)
complying with temperature constraints.
Item Coverage - Water stops
2.9
The items for water stops shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 1.N] and include for:

3. Refurbishment of existing bridge deck expansi...
3.
Refurbishment of existing bridge deck expansion joints
Definition
3.1
The term "bridge deck expansion joints" covers all types of permanent joints, which shall allow
expansion, contraction, shrinkage or angular rotation to take place in decks of structures.
Units
3.2
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
refurbishment of existing bridge deck expansion joints ............ number, linear metre or cubic
metre.
Measurement
3.3
The measurement of refurbishment of existing bridge deck expansion joints shall be the complete
replacement or repair, with the stated length measured along the centre line of the joint.
3.4
The measurement of refurbishment of existing bridge deck expansion joints incorporating asphaltic
material shall be for the replacing asphaltic material up to finished road level.
3.5
The measurement of refurbishment of existing bridge deck expansion joints measured as linear metres
and cubic metres shall be measured along the centre-line of the joint.
Itemisation
3.6
Separate items shall be provided for refurbishment of existing bridge deck expansion joints in
accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 2.N]and the following:
Table 3.6 Itemisation for Refurbishment of existing bridge deck expansion joints
Group
Feature
I
1 Refurbishment of existing bridge deck expansion joints.
II
1 Different types of materials.
III
1 Different lengths.
IV
1 Different gap widths.
Note: Group III Feature I shall only apply to stated lengths of bridge deck expansion joints.
Item Coverage - Refurbishment of existing bridge deck expansion joints
3.7
The items for refurbishment of existing bridge deck expansion joints shall be in accordance with clause
2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 1.N] and include for:
a)
bridge deck expansion joints as clause 1.7 of this document;
b)
protective systems for protection of steelwork against corrosion as clause 1.6 of CC 486/MMHW
[Ref 3.N];
c)
removal and disposal of existing joint arisings to tip off Site as clause 4.5 of GC 109/MMHW [Ref
4.N];
d)
provision, maintenance and removal of temporary bridging plates;
e)
other works and provisions for the renewal of joints.
