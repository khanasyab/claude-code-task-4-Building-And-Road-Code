Foreword
Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1. Bridge bearings
1.
Bridge bearings
Units
1.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
bridge bearings, installation of bridge bearings ............ number.
Measurement
1.2
The measurement shall be the complete unit.
Itemisation
1.3
Separate items shall be provided for bridge bearings in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of
GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 1.3 Itemisation for bridge bearings
Group
Feature
I
1 Bridge bearings.
2 Installation of bridge bearings.
II
1 Different types.
III
1 Different locations.
Item Coverage - Bridge bearings and installation of bridge bearings
1.4
The item coverage for bridge bearings and installation of bridge bearings shall be in accordance with
clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and include for:
a)
design in accordance with CD 350 [Ref 8.N], BS EN 1337-1 [Ref 7.N] and Contractor design in
Section 17 of GC 101 [Ref 1.N];
b)
reports and certificates;
c)
provision of data and drawings;
d)
re-submissions and modifications;
e)
amendments to the Works;
f)
obtaining approvals;
g)
everything necessary for the design, fabrication, installation and testing of the Works.

2. Refurbishment of existing bridge bearings
2.
Refurbishment of existing bridge bearings
Units
2.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
refurbishment of existing bridge bearings ............ number.
b)
jacking of existing bridge deck ............ item.
Measurement
2.2
The measurement of refurbishment of existing bridge bearings shall be the complete unit.
2.3
The measurement of jacking of existing bridge deck shall be for the complete installation and removal.
Note
The jacking of existing bridge deck is a temporary works item.
Itemisation
2.4
Separate items shall be provided for refurbishment of existing bridge bearings in accordance with
clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 2.4 Itemisation for Refurbishment of existing bridge bearings
Group
Feature
I
1 Refurbishment of existing bridge bearings.
2 Jacking of existing bridge deck.
II
1 Different types.
III
1 Different locations.
Item Coverage - Refurbishment of existing bridge bearings
2.5
The items for the replacement of bridge bearings shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref
2.N] and include for:
a)
inspections of existing bearings;
b)
masking and protection;
c)
setting out;
d)
provision of high pressure cleaning equipment;
e)
removal of loose corrosion products to bearing;
f)
removal of loose and flaking material to bearing case;
g)
excavation unacceptable earthworks material as clause 3.6 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 6.N];
h)
excavation of Hard Material as clause 4.4 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 6.N];
i)
disposal of unacceptable earthworks material as clause 7.6 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 6.N];
j)
replacement of existing bearings;
k)
protective systems for protection of steelwork against corrosion as clause 1.6 of CC 486/MMHW
[Ref 5.N];
l)
nuts, bolts, washers, dowels, protective caps, dust covers, sockets, sleeves, wrapping, adhesives
and lubricants;
m)
marking bearings for identification purposes.
