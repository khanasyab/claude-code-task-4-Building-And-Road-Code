Foreword
Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1. Footways, cycle tracks and paved areas
1.
Footways, cycle tracks and paved areas
Units
1.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
footways, cycle tracks and paved areas ............ square metre.
Measurement
1.2
The measurement of footways, cycle tracks and paved areas shall be calculated using the width of the
top surface stated in the Contract with no deductions made for openings of 1 square metre or less.
1.3
In the case of footways, cycle tracks and paved areas where a Group reference is given for the whole
construction, the total thickness of the combined capping, subbase, binder course and surface course
shall be stated.
1.4
In all other cases of footways, cycle tracks and paved areas the thickness of each course shall be
stated in the item description.
Itemisation
1.5
Separate items shall be provided for footways, cycle tracks and paved areas in accordance with
clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 6.N] and the following:
Table 1.5 Itemisation for Footways, cycle tracks and paved areas

2 Cycle tracks.
3 Paved areas.
II
1 Different types of construction.
III
1 Different thicknesses.
IV
1 Different sizes, groups or types.
V
1 Surfaces sloping at 10o or less to the horizontal.
2 Surfaces sloping at more than 10o to the horizontal.
Item Coverage - Footways, cycle tracks and paved areas
1.6

[Ref 5.N] and include for:

Kerb units, special kerb units, channels, edgings, combined kerb

Definitions

7.
Reinstatement of trenches and openings in footways, cycle tracks
and paved areas
Units
7.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
reinstatement of trenches and openings in footways, cycle tracks or paved areas ............ square
metre.
Measurement
7.2
The measurement of reinstatement of trenches and openings in footways, cycle tracks or paved areas
shall be calculated using the width of the top surface to be reinstated excluding sides and edges with
no deductions made for openings of 1 square metre or less.
7.3
The top surface for the following features shall be the widths or areas described below unless stated
otherwise:
a)
for drains, sewers, piped culverts, service ducts and filter drains - the width shall be the internal
diameter of the pipe plus 600 mm;
b)
for kerbs, channels, edgings, combined kerb drainage systems, linear drainage channel systems
and the like - the width of the foundations;
c)
for chambers, gullies, traffic signs, traffic signals, road lighting columns and the like - the
horizontal area of the base slab or where no base slab is required the area of the bottom of the
excavation.
Itemisation
7.4
Separate items shall be provided for reinstatement of trenches and openings in footways, cycle tracks
or paved areas in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 6.N] and the following:
