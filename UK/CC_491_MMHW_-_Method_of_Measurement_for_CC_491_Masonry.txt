Foreword
Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1. Brickwork
1.
Brickwork
Units
1.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
brickwork ............ square metre.
b)
copings, string courses and the like ............ linear metre.
Measurement
1.2
The measurement shall be the superficial area of brickwork required by the Contract with no deductions
made for openings of 0.10 square metre or less.
1.3
The measurement of copings, string courses and the like shall be the length of the work required by the
Contract.
1.4
Facings shall be measured as extra over brickwork except where brickwork is built entirely of facings.
Itemisation
1.5
Separate items shall be provided for brickwork in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112
[Ref 2.N] and the following:

3 In arches.
4 In alteration work.
Item Coverage - Brickwork
1.6
The items for brickwork, copings, string courses and the like shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of
GP 113 [Ref 1.N] and include for:
a)
bedding, jointing, pointing, raking out, wetting and fair-facedwork, including rough and fair cutting;
b)
plinths, corbels, bull-noses, chases, rebates, quoins and the like, grouting;
c)
ties, dowels, cramps, joggles and the like, including sinkings, mortices and running in;
d)
bonding into existing work;
e)
forming cavity;
f)
steel reinforcement as clause 5.8 of CC 482/MMHW [Ref 3.N];
g)
cavity filling between the brickwork and the backing;

2. Blockwork and stonework
2.
Blockwork and stonework
Units
2.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
blockwork and stonework ............ cubic metre.
b)
copings, string courses and the like ............ linear metre.
c)
individual blocks, features or stones ............ number.
Measurement
2.2
The measurement of blockwork and stonework shall be the volume of the work excluding the volume of
the cavity and associated filling.
2.3
The measurement of copings, string courses and the like shall be the length of the work required by the
Contract with no deductions made from the measurement for holes or voids of 0.15 cubic metre or less.
Itemisation
2.4
Separate items shall be provided for blockwork and stonework in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and
2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 2.N] and the following:

II

III
1 Different types of construction.
IV
1 Different materials.
V
1 Different types of masonry mortar.
VI
1 Curved on plan.
VII
1 With a battered face.
VIII
1 In walls.
2 In facework to concrete.
3 In arches.
4 In alteration work.
Item Coverage - Blockwork and stonework
2.5
The items for blockwork, stonework, copings, string courses and the like, individual blocks, features or
stones shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 1.N] and include for:
a)
dressing including in situ dressing;
b)
setting, bedding, jointing, coursing, raking out, quoins, grouting, pointing, wetting and fair-faced
work including rough and fair cutting;
c)
bonding into existing work;
d)
ties, dowels, cramps, joggles and the like including sinkings, mortices and running in;
e)
forming cavity;
f)
cavity filling between the blockwork or stonework and the backing;

3. Remove from store and relay brickwork, blockw...
3.
Remove from store and relay brickwork, blockwork and stonework
Units
3.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
remove from store and relay blockwork and stonework ............ cubic metre.
b)
remove from store and relay brickwork ............ square metre.
c)
remove from store and relay copings, string courses and the like ............ linear metre.
d)
remove from store and relay individual blocks, features or stones ............ number.
Measurement
3.2
The measurement of remove from store and relay blockwork and stonework shall be the volume of the
work required by the Contract with no deductions made for holes or voids of 0.15 cubic metre or less.
3.3
The measurement of remove from store and relay brickwork shall be the superficial area of re-used
brickwork required by the Contract with no deductions made for openings of 0.1 square metre or less.
3.4
The measurement of remove from store and relay copings, string courses and the like shall be the
length of the work required by the Contract.
3.5
Brick facings shall be measured as extra over remove from store and relay brickwork except where the
brickwork is built entirely of facings.
Itemisation
3.6
Separate items shall be provided for remove from store and relay brickwork, blockwork and stonework
in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 2.N]and the following:

Item Coverage - Remove from store and relay brickwork, blockwork and stonework
3.7
The items for remove from store and relay brickwork, blockwork and stonework shall be in accordance
with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 1.N] and include for:
a)
loading, transporting from store unloading and positioning for relaying;
b)
replacing items damaged during the foregoing operation;
c)
modifications and new materials;
d)
brickwork, copings, string courses and the like as clause 1.6 of this document;
e)
blockwork, stonework, copings, string courses and the like, individual blocks, features or stones
as clause 2.5 of this document.

4. Repointing brickwork, blockwork and stonework
4.
Repointing brickwork, blockwork and stonework
Units
4.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
repointing brickwork, blockwork and stonework ............ square metre.
Measurement
4.2
The measurement shall be the superficial area of brickwork, blockwork and stonework required by the
Contract.
4.3
The measurement of arch soffits shall be the developed plan area.
Itemisation
4.4
Separate items shall be provided for repointing brickwork, blockwork and stonework in accordance with
clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 2.N]and the following:
Table 4.4 Itemisation for Repointing brickwork, blockwork and stonework
Group
Feature
I
1 Repointing.
II
1 Brickwork.
2 Blockwork.
3 Stonework.
III
1 Different types of masonry mortar.
IV
1 In walls.
2 In facework to concrete.
3 In arches.
4 In alteration work.
Item Coverage - Repointing brickwork, blockwork and stonework
4.5
The items for repointing brickwork, blockwork and stonework shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of
GP 113 [Ref 1.N] and include for:
a)
raking out existing unsound mortar;
b)
grinding out joints;
c)
saturation of open joints prior to repointing;
d)
low pressure water jetting, including provisions, collection and disposal of water;
e)
cleaning faces of brickwork or stonework;
f)
taking up individual loose bricks, blocks or stones and re-bedding;
g)
disposal of unacceptable earthworks material as clause 7.6 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 5.N].
