Foreword
Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1. Survey of existing features and/or areas of s...
1.
Survey of existing features and/or areas of site
Units
1.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
survey ............ number.
Itemisation
1.2
Separate items shall be provided for a survey of existing features and/or areas of site in accordance
with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 1.2 Itemisation for Survey of existing features and/or areas of Sste
Group
Feature
I
1 Survey of existing features and/or areas of site.
2 Baseline survey for trenchless crossings.
Item coverage - Survey of existing features and/or areas of site
1.3
The items for survey of existing features and/or areas of site shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of
GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and include for:
a)
survey report;
b)
control report;
c)
survey data;
d)
revisions and updates;
e)
compliance with all relevant requirements as set out in the Design Manual of Roads and Bridges
(DMRB) documents GG 951 [Ref 1.N] and GS 952 [Ref 6.N].

2. Scheme billboards
2.
Scheme billboards
Units
2.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
scheme billboard ............ number.
Itemisation
2.2
Separate items shall be provided for scheme billboards in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of
GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 2.2 Itemisation for Scheme billboards
Group
Feature
I
1 Scheme billboard.
II
1 Different types.
III
1 Different sizes.
Item coverage - Scheme billboards
2.3
The items for scheme billboards shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and include
for:
a)
excavation of acceptable earthworks material as clauses 3.2 and 3.3 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 5.N];
b)
excavation of unacceptable earthworks material as clause 3.6 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 5.N];
c)
excavation in Hard Material as clause 4.4 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 5.N];
d)
backfilling and compaction;
e)
in situ concrete as clause 1.4 of CC 482/MMHW [Ref 4.N];
f)
formwork as clause 3.9 of CC 482/MMHW [Ref 4.N];
g)
disposal of unacceptable earthworks material as clause 7.6 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 5.N];
h)
painting, reflectorisation and illumination;
i)
inspections, cleaning, maintaining and repairing;
j)
dismantling and removing from Site;
k)
reinstatement of surfaces;
l)
re-positioning.
