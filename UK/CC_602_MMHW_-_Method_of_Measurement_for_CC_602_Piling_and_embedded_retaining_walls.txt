1. Piling plant
1.
Piling plant
Note
Piling platforms are to be measured as temporary works (indirect cost item) and therefore do not form
part of item coverage in this method of measurement.
Units
1.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
establishment of piling plant ............ item.
b)
moving piling plant ............ number.
Measurement
1.2
The establishment of piling plant shall be measured once only to each structure.
1.3
Any additional establishment of piling plant to suit the Contractor's method of working shall not be
measured.
1.4
The measurement of moving piling plant shall be measured once only to each pile.
1.5
Moving of piling plant shall not be measured for steel sheet piling.
Itemisation
1.6
Separate items shall be provided for piling plant in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112

2. Driven precast concrete piles
2.
Driven precast concrete piles
Units
2.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
precast concrete piles, driving, lengthening, driving lengthened piles ............ linear metre.
b)
stripping precast concrete pile heads ............ number.
Measurement
2.2
The measurement of driven precast concrete piles shall be the lengths required by the Contract.
2.3
The jointing of segmental piles shall not be measured.
2.4
The measurement of driving precast concrete piles shall be the length of each pile measured along the
axis from the toe to:
a)
the Existing Ground Level or the level of the underside of the pile cap or ground beam (ignoring
any blinding layer) whichever is the lower, provided that where a particular level is specified from
which driving shall commence then the measurement shall be to that specified level; or
b)
the site joint of piles to be lengthened, provided that the site joint after completion of the driving is
below the level determined in accordance with the preceding sub- paragraph.
2.5
The measurement of lengthening precast concrete piles shall be the lengths of the added concrete
ordered by the Overseeing Organisation.
2.6
The measurement of lengthening precast segmental piles shall be the length of the added segment
ordered by the Overseeing Organisation.
2.7
The measurement of driving lengthened precast concrete piles shall be the length from the site joint to
the level determined in accordance with clause 2.4 a) of this document above.
2.8
The length classification for those items listed in clause 2.1 a) of this document above shall be based
on the appropriate measured lengths determined in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, 2.4, 2.5, 2.6, and
2.7 of this document above inclusive.

3. Cast-in-place piles
3.
Cast-in-place piles
Units
3.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
pile shafts, empty bores ............ linear metre.
b)
enlarged bases, heads ............ number.
Measurement
3.2
The measurement of pile shafts shall be the length of the pile measured along the axis from the toe of
the shoe or the bottom of the excavation, including any enlarged base, whichever is appropriate to the
specified finished level of the concrete pile head.
3.3
Where an empty bore is specified above a pile shaft, the length classification of the pile shaft shall be
based on the overall bored or driven depth including the empty bore.
3.4
Empty bores shall only be measured from a specified commencing level.
3.5
Empty bores shall be the length of empty bore or drive measured from the specified finished level of the
concrete pile head, to that particular commencing level.
Itemisation
3.6

4. Steel reinforcement for cast-in-place piles
4.
Steel reinforcement for cast-in-place piles
Units
4.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
bar and helical reinforcement ............ tonne.
Measurement
4.2
The mass of plain bar reinforcement to cast-in-place piles shall be calculated on the basis that the
nominal density of steel is 0.00785 kilogrammes per square millimetre of cross-sectional area per linear
metre.
4.3
The mass of deformed bar reinforcement shall be calculated on the basis of the nominal rolling mass of
the reinforcement.

6. Steel bearing piles
6.
Steel bearing piles
Units
6.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
steel bearing piles, driving, lengthening pieces, driving lengthened steel bearing piles ............
linear metre.
b)
welding on lengthening pieces ............ number.
c)
cutting or burning off surplus ............ number.
Measurement
6.2
The measurement of steel bearing piles shall be the length required by the Contract.
6.3
The measurement of driving steel bearing piles shall be the length of the pile measured along the axis
from the toe to:
a)
the Existing Ground Level or the level of the underside of the pile cap or ground beam (ignoring

6.5

level determined in accordance with clause 6.3 a) of this document above.
6.6
The length classification for those items listed in clause 6.1 a) of this document above shall be based
on the measured lengths determined in accordance with clauses 6.2, 6.3, 6.4, and 6.5 of this document
above inclusive.
Note
If it is anticipated that the final lengths of the steel bearing piles may differ from those originally required
in the Contract then items for lengthening or shortening should be included. In the case of cutting or
burning off surplus, the number admeasured should be of those piles reaching a set at a length shorter
than originally required and accepted by the Overseeing Organisation. The item for cutting or burning
off surplus piles applies to both steel bearing piles and lengthened steel bearing piles.
