Foreword
Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1. Road lighting columns and brackets, wall moun...
1.
Road lighting columns and brackets, wall mountings, steel CCTV
masts, steel cantilever masts and steel high masts
Units
1.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
road lighting columns and brackets, wall mountings, steel CCTV masts, steel cantilever masts
and steel high masts ............ number.
Measurement
1.2
The measurement of road lighting columns and brackets, wall mountings, steel CCTV masts, steel
cantilever masts and steel high masts, shall be the complete installation.
1.3
Earth electrodes shall be measured separately under clauses 8.1, 8.2, 8.2 Note, 8.3, and 8.4 of
TC 501/MMHW [Ref 11.N].
Itemisation
1.4
Separate items shall be provided for road lighting columns and brackets, wall mountings, steel CCTV
masts, steel cantilever masts and steel high masts in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of
GP 112 [Ref 5.N] and the following:
Table 1.4 Itemisation for Road lighting columns and brackets, wall mountings, steel CCTV
masts, steel cantilever masts and steel high masts
Group
Feature
I
1 Road lighting columns and brackets.
2 Wall mountings.
3 Steel CCTV masts.
4 Steel cantilever masts.
5 Steel high masts.
II
1 Different height of lighting columns.
2 Different height of steel CCTV masts.
3 Different height of steel cantilever masts.
4 Different height of steel high masts.
III
1 Different projection of brackets.
2 Different projection of cantilever.
IV
1 Different luminaires.
V
1 Different types.
VI
1 Components supplied by the Overseeing Organisation.
Item Coverage - Road lighting columns and brackets, wall mountings, steel CCTV masts, steel
cantilever masts and steel high masts
1.5
The items for road lighting columns and brackets, wall mountings, steel CCTV masts, steel cantilever
masts and steel high masts shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 4.N] and include for:
a)
excavation of acceptable earthworks material as clauses 3.4 and 3.5 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 8.N];
b)
excavation of unacceptable earthworks material as clause 3.6 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 8.N];
c)
excavation in Hard Material as clause 4.4 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 8.N];
d)
blinding concrete and paving slab;

6. Noise barrier
6.
Noise barrier
Units
6.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
noise barrier ............ linear metre.
b)
noise barrier gate ............ number.
Measurement
6.2
The measurement of noise barrier shall be the developed length along the centre line of the barrier.
6.3
The measurement of height of barrier shall be that stated in the Contract for the type of barrier.
6.4
The measurement of width of noise barrier gates shall be the distance between the outer edges of the
stiles; the outer edges of hanging stiles in the case of double gates.
6.5
The measurement of height of noise barrier gates shall be the distance between the upper surface of
the top rail and the underside of the bottom rail.
Itemisation
6.6
Separate items shall be provided for noise barrier in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of
GP 112 [Ref 5.N] and the following:
Table 6.6 Itemisation for Noise barrier
Group
Feature
I
1 Each type of noise barrier.
2 Each type of noise barrier gate.
II
1 Barriers of different heights.
2 Gates of different heights and widths.
Item Coverage - Noise barrier
6.7
The items for noise barrier shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 4.N] and include for:
a)
compliance with specified design criteria;
b)
fencing as clause 1.11 of LC 127/MMHW [Ref 10.N];
c)
all foundation types;
d)
concrete foundations to posts as clause 1.12 of LC 127/MMHW [Ref 10.N];
e)
prefabricated concrete foundations as clause 2.5 of CC 482/MMHW [Ref 6.N].
Item Coverage - Noise barrier designed by the Contractor
6.8
The respective coverage rules for noise barrier above shall be replaced and/or supplemented with the
items below where noise barrier, or elements and components thereof is to be designed by the
Contractor.
6.9

a)
design in accordance with CD 354 [Ref 1.N], CD 372 [Ref 2.N], Sections 17 and 18 of GC 101
[Ref 3.N];
