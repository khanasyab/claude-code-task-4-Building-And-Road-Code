1. Locating buried road lighting and illuminated...
1.
Locating buried road lighting and illuminated traffic signs cable
Units
1.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
locating buried road lighting and illuminated traffic signs cable ............ linear metre.
Measurement
1.2
The measurement shall be the length of the buried road lighting or illuminated traffic signs cable
located as clause 6.2 of this document for the measurement of cable.
1.3
The measurement of quantities for locating buried cables shall only be applied to those cases where
the Overseeing Organisation requires a length of cable to be located and not to the situation where an
end or particular point has to be found to facilitate a connection or joint. The latter is covered by clause
2.1 h) of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] within the Preambles to Bill of Quantities and various references within item
coverages.
Itemisation
1.4
Separate items shall be provided for locating buried road lighting and illuminated traffic signs cable in
accordance withclauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 1.4 Itemisation for Locating buried road lighting and illuminated traffic signs cable
Group
Feature
I
1 Locating buried road lighting and illuminated traffic signs cable.
II
1 In carriageways, footways, bridge decks and paved areas.
2 In verges and central reserves.
3 In side slopes of cuttings or side slopes of embankments.
Item Coverage - Locating buried road lighting and illuminated traffic signs cable
1.5
The items for locating buried road lighting and illuminated traffic signs cable shall be in accordance with
clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and include for:
a)
marking the cable runs on the ground;
b)
pegging and removal;
c)
maintaining location system;
d)
proving insulation integrity.

5. Trench for power cable or power cable duct
5.
Trench for power cable or power cable duct
Units
5.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
trench for power cable ............ linear metre.
b)
trench for power cable duct ............ linear metre.
Measurement
5.2
The measurement of trench for power cable or power cable duct shall be the summation of the distance
along the centre line of the route between the following points:
a)
face of foundation to post, road lighting column, lit sign unit, feeder pillar, electricity supply point
and the like;
b)
the intersection of the centre line at junctions;
c)
the position of terminations shown in the Contract;
d)
the point of change of stage depth.
5.3
The measurement of trench for power cable or power cable duct shall be measured once only
irrespective of the number of cables or ducts in the trench.
5.4
The depth of power cable or power cable duct trenches shall be the vertical measurement between the
underside of the bedding, or if no bedding is specified the underside of the cable or duct, and the
Earthworks Outline.
Itemisation
5.5
Separate items shall be provided for trench for power cable or power cable duct in accordance with

6. Power cable and power cable duct
6.
Power cable and power cable duct
Units
6.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
power cable ............ linear metre.
b)
power cable duct ............ linear metre.
Measurement
6.2
The measurement of power cable shall be the summation of the individual lengths along the centre line
of the route of the cable between the points of cable termination within each item of equipment.
6.3
The points of power cable termination shall be:
a)
in the case of loop detector feeders - the point at which the cable enters the terminal block;
b)
in all other cases - the point at which the cables enter the boxes, distributors, lighting columns,
cut-outs and the like.
6.4
The measurement of power cable ducts shall be for the complete construction irrespective of the
number of ducts contained within any one trench.

8. Earth electrodes
8.
Earth electrodes
Note
Lightning protection systems for roadside technology equipment and gantries are measured in TC
131/MMHW [Ref 11.N].
Units
8.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
earth electrodes ............ number.
Measurement
8.2
The measurement of earth electrodes shall be the complete installation including conduit and cable
between the structure, nearest Lit Sign Unit or lighting unit and the earth electrode.
Note
The exact number of electrodes required is determined according to the results of resistivity surveys on
the soils which will be in contact with the electrodes. Such surveys cannot be done at tender stage
where electrodes are to be installed on embankments or in the bottoms of deep cuttings and it is
suggested that the quantities billed in such cases are marked "provisional".

9. Chambers
9.
Chambers
Units
9.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
chambers and modification to existing chambers ............ number.
Measurement
9.2
The measurement shall be of the complete chamber.
9.3
Depths of chambers shall be the distance between the top surfaces of the cover and the uppermost
surface of the base slab.
9.4
Where no base slab is required the depth of chambers shall be taken to the bottom of the excavation.
9.5
Modification shall be measured once only per chamber.
Itemisation
9.6
Separate items shall be provided for chambers and modification to existing chambers in accordance
with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 9.6 Itemisation for Chambers and modification to existing chambers

Feature
