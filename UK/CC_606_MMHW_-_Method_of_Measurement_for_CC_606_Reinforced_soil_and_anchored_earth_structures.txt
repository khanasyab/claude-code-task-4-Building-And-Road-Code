Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1. Reinforced soil and anchored earth structures...
1.
Reinforced soil and anchored earth structures designed by the
Contractor
1.1
This method of measurement shall be used only when the Contract provides for reinforced soil and
anchored earth structures to be full, or part designed by the Contractor.
1.2
Notwithstanding the provisions for measurement of the Works set forth elsewhere all the Works (with
the exception of those works scheduled as not to be included) within the Designated Outlines shall be
deemed to be measured and included within the single items in the Bill of Quantities priced by the
Contractor.
1.3
The measurement of the remainder of the Works shall exclude all Works contained within the
Designated Outlines except for those works scheduled as stated above.
Note
A definition and guidance on the application of Designated Outlines is provided in clause 1.1 of GP 112
[Ref 3.N].
1.4
Structural elements or aspects of work that are not Contractor designed, and which are measured and
priced within this measurement section, shall be subject to same corresponding item coverage included
elsewhere within the MMHW.
Units
1.5
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
reinforced soil and anchored earth structures designed by the Contractor ............ item.
Measurement
1.6
The measurement of reinforced soil and anchored earth structures full or part designed by the
Contractor shall be the complete construction within the Designated Outlines with the exception of
those works scheduled as not to be included.
Itemisation
1.7
Separate items shall be provided for reinforced soil and anchored earth structures full or part designed
by the Contractor in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
