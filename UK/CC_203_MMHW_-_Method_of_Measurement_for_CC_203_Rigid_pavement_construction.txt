Foreword
Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1. Rigid pavement construction
1.
Rigid pavement construction
Units
1.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
base, surface slab ............ square metre.
Measurement
1.2
The measurement of base and surface slab shall be calculated using the width of the top surface of the
course or slab with no deductions made for openings of 1 square metre or less.
1.3
The width of the "top surface" of the base or surface slab shall be the width required by the Contract,
excluding sloping sides or edges.
Itemisation
1.4
Separate items shall be provided for base and surface slab in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and
2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 2.N] and the following:
Table 1.4 Itemisation for Rigid pavement construction

Note: For each item which includes Group VII Feature 1, an associated item shall be provided for

Item Coverage - Rigid pavement construction
1.5
The items for base and surface slab shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 1.N] and

e)
air entrainment;
f)
