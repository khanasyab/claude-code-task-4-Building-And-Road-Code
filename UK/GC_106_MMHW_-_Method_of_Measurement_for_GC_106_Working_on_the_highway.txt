Foreword
Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

2. Temporary traffic management
2.
Temporary traffic management
Temporary traffic management
Units
2.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
temporary traffic management for all works ............ item.
b)
taking measures for or construction, maintenance, removal of contraflow arrangements ............
item.
Note
The items above are included to enable the measurement and lump sum pricing only of temporary
traffic management and taking measures for or construction, maintenance, removal of contraflow
arrangements.
Measurement
2.2
Temporary traffic management for all works shall be measured once only.
2.3
Unless stated otherwise in the Contract, taking measures for or construction, maintenance, removal of
contraflow arrangements shall be measured once only to include for all contraflow arrangements
specified in Section 1 of GC 106 [Ref 9.N] and all contraflow arrangements proposed by the Contractor.
Itemisation
2.4
Separate items shall be provided for temporary management in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and
2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 4.N] and the following:
Table 2.4 Itemisation for Temporary traffic management
Group
Feature
I
1 Temporary traffic management for all works.
2 Taking measures for or construction, maintenance, removal of contraflow
arrangements.
Note
The features for 'Amend closure' and 'Re-establishment of closure' are included for continuous works in
verges, where cones are moved in and out at the end or start of each shift e.g. to amend a hard
shoulder closure to a lane one closure and vice versa.

Temporary traffic management closures
Units
2.7
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
establishment, re-establishment, removal, establishment and removal, amend and adjustment of
temporary traffic management closures ............ item.
b)
maintenance and adjustment for maintenance of temporary traffic management closures ............

Measurement
2.8

closures shall be the complete installation or removal of each closure.
2.9
The measurement of maintenance of closures shall be each day the closure is provided.
2.10
The measurement of adjustment of closures shall be the complete installation or removal for the stated
adjustment length.
