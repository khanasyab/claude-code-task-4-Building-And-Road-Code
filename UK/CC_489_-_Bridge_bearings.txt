Requirements completed by the Specifier.

specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.

1. General requirements for bridge bearings
1.
General requirements for bridge bearings
1.1
Bridge bearings shall be Contractor design items.
1.2
The design of bridge bearings shall be in accordance with BS EN 1337-1 [Ref 9.N] and CD 350 [Ref
19.N].
1.3
The requirements for "Contractor design" in Section 17 of GC 101 [Ref 1.N] shall apply to bridge
bearings.
1.4
Bridge bearings shall be as specified in CC 489/WSR/001.
Product requirements for bridge bearings
1.5
All bridge bearings shall be in accordance with BS EN 1337-1 [Ref 9.N], supplemented by PD 6703
[Ref 7.N] and the applicable standards listed in table 1.5
Table 1.5 Bridge bearings and their applicable standards
Type of bridge bearings
Standard
Sliding elements
BS EN 1337-2 [Ref 16.N]
Elastomeric bearings
BS EN 1337-3 [Ref 8.N]
Roller bearings
BS EN 1337-4 [Ref 15.N]
Pot bearings
BS EN 1337-5 [Ref 12.N]
Rocker bearings
BS EN 1337-6 [Ref 14.N]
Spherical and cylindrical PTFE bearings
BS EN 1337-7 [Ref 17.N]
Guided bearings
BS EN 1337-8 [Ref 10.N]
1.6
Bridge bearings shall meet the performance characteristics listed in the bearing schedule referenced in
CC 489/WSR/001 and in the standard applicable for the type of bridge bearing listed in table 1.5.
1.7
The requirements for Designated standards in Section 10 of GC 101 [Ref 1.N] shall apply to bridge
bearings.
Installation, storage and transportation requirements for bridge bearings
1.8
The transportation, storage and installation of bridge bearings shall be in accordance with BS EN
1337-1 [Ref 9.N], BS EN 1337-11 [Ref 18.N], PD 6703 [Ref 7.N] and the manufacturer's instructions.
Documentation requirements for bridge bearings
1.9
The following Documentation shall be submitted for bridge bearings prior to the commencement of
bearing installation: completed bridge bearing schedule.
1.10
The requirements for "Documentation" in Section 2 of GC 101 [Ref 1.N] shall apply to the completed
bridge bearing schedule.
Verification requirements for bridge bearings installation
1.11
Verification shall be undertaken for bridge bearing installation by inspection in accordance with BS EN
1337-10 [Ref 11.N], CS 450 [Ref 2.N] and the bridge bearing manufacturer's requirements.
1.12
The frequency of bridge bearing installation inspection shall be once upon completion of bridge bearing
installation.
1.13
The requirements for "Verification" in Section 14 of GC 101 [Ref 1.N] shall apply to bridge bearing
installation inspection.

1. General requirements for bridge bearings
Documentation requirements for bridge bearings installation
1.14
The following Documentation shall be submitted for the installation of each bridge bearing upon
completion of the bearing installation: Bridge Bearing Information Pack.
1.15
The requirements for Documentation in Section 2 of GC 101 shall apply to the Bridge Bearing
Information Pack.
1.16
The Bridge Bearing Information Pack shall include:
1)
reports and records on transportation, storage and installations as required in BS EN 1337-11
[Ref 18.N]; and,
2)
information on future maintenance.
1.17
The information on future maintenance of the bridge bearings in the Bearing Information Pack shall
include:
1)
requirements for the bearings to be replaced in the future;
2)
details of how to remove, maintain or replace protection measures for inspection and/or
maintenance of the bearing;
3)
removal and re-setting methods including sequences and timings;
4)
specific maintenance requirements, including cleaning, grease, measurements, and the required
intervals.

2.
Surface preparation and protection against corrosion for bridge
bearings
2.1
The surface preparation and corrosion protection for steel in bridge bearings shall comply with BS EN
1337-9 [Ref 13.N] and CC 486 [Ref 6.N].
2.2
Surface preparation for steel in bridge bearings shall comply with "Protective systems for steel in bridge
bearings and metal coated fasteners for protection of steelwork against corrosion" in Section 6 of
CC 486 [Ref 6.N].
Surface preparation and corrosion protection on existing structures where new
bearings are to be installed
2.3
The surface preparation and corrosion protection for steelwork on existing structures where new bridge
bearings are to be installed shall comply with CM 431 [Ref 3.N] and CC 487 [Ref 4.N].
Verification of surface preparation and protection against corrosion for bridge
bearings
2.4
Verification shall be undertaken for compliance of the surface preparation through visual inspection by
competent person's as given in CC 486 [Ref 6.N].
2.5
The frequency of compliance inspection of the surface preparation shall be prior to application of each
coat of the paint system.
2.6
The requirements for "Verification" in Section 14 of GC 101 [Ref 1.N] shall apply to compliance
inspections of the standard of surface preparation.
2.7
Verification for testing of corrosion protection coating shall be undertaken by an accredited testing
laboratory in compliance with "Accredited laboratory" in Section 16 of GC 101 [Ref 1.N].
2.8
Verification for testing of corrosion protection coating shall comply with CC 486 [Ref 6.N].
Documentation requirements for surface preparation for protection of steelwork
against corrosion
2.9
The following Documentation shall be submitted for surface preparation prior to the commencement of
the application of paint system: visual inspection report as required in CC 486 [Ref 6.N].
2.10
The requirements for "Documentation" in Section 2 of GC 101 [Ref 1.N] shall apply to reports for the
visual inspection of surface preparation.
