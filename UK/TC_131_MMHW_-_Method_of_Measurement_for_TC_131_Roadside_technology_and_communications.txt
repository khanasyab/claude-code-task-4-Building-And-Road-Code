Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1.
Locating buried communications and power cable
Note
All references to power within this method of measurement are for electrical installations solely for the
use of roadside technology and communications.
Units
1.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
locating buried communications cable ............ linear metre.
b)
locating buried power cable ............ linear metre.
c)
locating buried communications and power cable ............ linear metre.
Measurement
1.2
The measurement shall be the length of the buried communications and power cable located as clause
4.2 of this document for the measurement of cable.
1.3
The measurement of quantities for locating buried cables shall only be applied to those cases where
the Overseeing Organisation requires a length of cable to be located and not to the situation where an
end or particular point has to be found to facilitate a connection or joint. The latter is covered by clause
2.1 h) of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] within the Preambles to Bill of Quantities and various references within item
coverages.
Itemisation
1.4
Separate items shall be provided for locating buried communications and power cable in accordance
with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 1.4 Itemisation for Locating buried communications and power cable
Group
Feature
I
1 Locating buried communications cable.
2 Locating buried power cable.
3 Locating buried communications and power cable.
II
1 In carriageways, footways, bridge decks and paved areas.
2 In verges and central reserves.
3 In side slopes of cuttings or side slopes of embankments.
Item Coverage - Locating buried communications and power cable
1.5
The items for locating buried communications and power cable shall be in accordance with clause 2.1
of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and include for:
a)
marking the cable runs on the ground;
b)
collection of digital and GIS information;
c)
location system and its removal;
d)
maintaining location system;
e)
proving insulation integrity;
f)
trial pits as clause 34.4 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 10.N].

3.
Trenches for cables and ducts for roadside technology and
communications
Units
3.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
trench for communications cable ............ linear metre.
b)
trench for power cable ............ linear metre.
c)
trench for communications and power cable ............ linear metre.
d)
trench for communications duct ............ linear metre.
e)
trench for power duct ............ linear metre.
f)
trench for communications and power duct ............ linear metre.
Measurement
3.2
The measurement of trench for communications and power cable or duct shall be the summation of the
distance along the centre line of the route between the following points:
a)
face of foundation to cabinet, post, electricity interface (EI) and the like;
b)
the intersection of the centre line at junctions;
c)
the position of terminations shown in the Contract;
d)
the internal faces of chambers;
e)
the point of change of stage depth.
3.3
The measurement of trench for communications and power cable or duct shall be measured once only
irrespective of the number of cables or ducts in the trench.
3.4
The depth of cable or duct trenches shall be the vertical measurement between the underside of the
bedding, or if no bedding is specified the underside of the cable or duct, and the Earthworks Outline.

3.5
Separate items shall be provided for trench for communications and power cable or duct in accordance
with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:

4.
Communications and power cable and communications and
power duct
Units
4.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
communications cable ............ linear metre.
b)
power cable ............ linear metre.
c)
communications duct ............ linear metre.
d)
power duct ............ linear metre.
Measurement
4.2
The measurement of communications and power cable shall be the summation of the individual lengths
along the centre line of the route of the cable between the points of cable termination within each item
of equipment.
4.3
Points of cable termination shall be:
a)
in the case of loop detector feeders - the point at which the cable enters the terminal block;
b)
in all other cases - the point at which the cables enter the boxes, distributors and the like.
4.4
The measurement of communications and power duct shall be for the complete construction
irrespective of the number of ducts contained within any one trench.
4.5
Where more than one communications and power duct is laid in a trench then the number of ducts shall
be stated in the item description.
Itemisation
4.6
Separate items shall be provided for communications and power cable and communications and power
duct in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
