Foreword
Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

2.
Pesticide use on landscape and ecological work
Units
2.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
weed control to hard standings and paved areas, topsoil heaps, planted beds, open ditches, filter
drains, grassed areas and waterbodies ............ square metre.
b)
weed control to individual trees and shrubs ............ number.
c)
control and removal of injurious and invasive weeds ............ square metre.
d)
make safe areas of invasive pests, either through the use of biological solution or by cutting out
the affected vegetation to hard standings and paved areas, topsoil heaps, planted beds, open
ditches, filter drains, grassed areas and waterbodies ............ square metre.
e)
make safe areas of invasive pests, either through the use of biological solution or by cutting out
affected vegetation to individual trees and shrubs ............ number.
Measurement
2.2
The measurement of weed control to hard standings and paved areas, topsoil heaps, planted beds,
open ditches, filter drains and grassed areas shall be the plan area.
2.3
The measurement of weed control and removal of injurious and invasive weeds shall be the plan area.
2.4
The measurement of weed control to individual trees and shrubs shall be the number of trees or shrubs
irrespective of girth.
2.5
Weed control shall be measured separately only when undertaken in connection with any landscape
and ecology areas that have been established prior to the commencement of the Contract.
2.6
The measurement of make safe areas of invasive pests to hard standings and paved areas, topsoil
heaps, planted beds, open ditches, filter drains, grassed areas and waterbodies shall be the plan area.
2.7
The measurement of make safe areas of invasive pests to individual trees and shrubs shall be the
number of trees or shrubs irrespective of girth.
2.8
Make safe areas of invasive pests shall be measured separately only when undertaken in connection
with any landscape and ecology areas that have been established prior to the commencement of the
Contract.
