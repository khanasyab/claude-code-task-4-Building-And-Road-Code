Foreword
Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1. Deliver severe weather service activities
1.
Deliver severe weather service activities
Units
1.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
deliver severe weather service activities ............ sum.
Note
Severe weather service activities are typically priced at twelve monthly interventions.
Measurement
1.2
The measurement of severe weather service activities shall be for the whole period required by the
Service Manager.
1.3
Stated seasonal periods shall add to a sum of twelve months duration per year.
Itemisation
1.4
Separate items shall be provided to deliver the severe weather service activities in accordance with
clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 2.N] and the following:
Table 1.4 Itemisation for Deliver severe weather service activities
Group
Feature
I
1 Take delivery of salt.
2 Salt management.
3 Provision of drivers on standby.
4 Provision, management and maintenance of equipment.
5 Provision of fuel and all necessary materials to deliver severe weather service.
6 Provision of storage for severe weather materials.
7 Maintain winter fleet.
8 Manage brine.
9 Maintain saturator.
10 Reporting for severe weather service.
11 Other severe weather service duties.
II
1 Stated seasonal period.
Item Coverage - Deliver severe weather service activities
1.5
The items for deliver severe weather service activities shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113
[Ref 1.N] and include for:
a)
collaborating with the Overseeing Organisation to develop a severe weather plan;
b)
providing all measures, equipment, resources and arrangements necessary to ensure
compliance with the specification and severe weather plan plan including design of salt routes,
design of the snow clearing routes, provision and management of salt stocks and the provision of
equipment at each designated loading point provided by the Contractor;
c)
all costs of equipment to vehicles and plant with ploughs and inserts, blowers and throwers;
d)
routine maintenance operations to the Overseeing Organisation's snow ploughs, during and
outside seasonal periods;
e)
training of operatives with operational vehicles with relevant certification;
f)
protection of Overseeing Organisation's and Contractor's equipment;
g)
24 hour availability of equipment to a serviceable condition;
h)
provision of a responsible person to receive the Service Manager's instructions on winter
maintenance and to ensure satisfactory compliance with any such instructions;

2. Preparation for and precautionary salting
2.
Preparation for and precautionary salting
Units
2.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
preparation for precautionary salting ............ depot.
b)
precautionary salting ............ kilometre or salting route.
c)
extra over precautionary salting ............ kilometre or salting route.
Measurement
2.2
The measurement of preparation for precautionary salting shall be per depot preparation period from
the decision to treat, to load the salt and brine until all the Winter Service Vehicles required are loaded,
manned and ready to leave the depot.
2.3
The measurement of precautionary salting shall be the number of salting routes or gritting lengths
instructed by the Service Manager.
Note
Salting routes may include for the spraying of potassium acetate liquid on bridges or sensitive
structures.
Itemisation
2.4
Separate items shall be provided for preparation for precautionary salting and precautionary salting in
accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 2.N] and the following:
Table 2.4 Itemisation for Preparation for and precautionary salting
Group
Feature
I
1 Preparation for precautionary salting.
2 Precautionary salting.
3 Extra over precautionary salting for increased spread rates.
II
1 Primary routes.
2 Secondary routes.
III
1 Stated time period.
Item Coverage - Preparation of precautionary salting
2.5
The items for preparation for precautionary salting shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113
[Ref 1.N] and include for:
a)
determination of the extent of the gritting operation and agreement with the Overseeing
Organisation;
b)
loading of the salt/brine, supplied by the Overseeing Organisation;
c)
calculating the amount of salt/brine required by each vehicle.
Item Coverage - Precautionary salting
2.6
The items for precautionary salting shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 1.N] and
include for:
a)
carrying out pre-operational checks on vehicle in accordance with the scope and record results;
b)
operating the vehicle in accordance with the scope;
c)
working out of sequence due to temporary traffic management (TTM) measures;
d)
full wash down after salting;

3. Fitting and removal of snow plough blades
3.
Fitting and removal of snow plough blades
Unit
3.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
fitting of snow plough blades ............ number.
b)
removal of snow plough blades ............ number.
Measurement
3.2
The measurement of fitting of snow plough blades shall be the number of snow plough blades required
and instructed to be fitted by the Overseeing Organisation.
3.3
The measurement of removal of snow plough blades shall be the number of snow plough blades
required and instructed to be removed by the Overseeing Organisation.
Itemisation
3.4
Separate items shall be provided for fitting and removal of snow plough blades in accordance with
clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 2.N] and the following:
Table 3.4 Itemisation for Fitting and removal of snow plough blades
Group
Feature
I
1 Fitting of snow plough blades.
2 Removal of snow plough blades.
Item Coverage - Fitting of snow plough blades
3.5
The items for fitting of snow plough blades shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 1.N]
and include for:
a)
taking snow plough blade from store and fitting to the vehicle in accordance with the
manufacturers recommendations;
b)
connecting hydraulic hoses and tightening bolts to the specified torque settings in accordance
with the manufacturers recommendations;
c)
fixing consumables and fixtures;
d)
repairs;
e)
replacement of damaged or stolen parts;
f)
cleaning;
g)
repositioning.
Item Coverage - Removal of snow plough blades
3.6
The items for removal of snow plough blades shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref
1.N] and include for:
a)
disconnecting hydraulic hoses and tightening bolts to the specified torque settings in accordance
with the manufacturers recommendations;
b)
removal of snow plough blade in accordance with the manufacturers recommendations and
taking back to store;
c)
fixing consumables and fixtures;
d)
repairs;
e)
replacement of damaged or stolen parts;

4. Winter service duties
4.
Winter service duties
Units
4.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
operatives for winter service duties ............ hour.
b)
equipment for winter service duties ............ hour.
c)
materials for winter service duties ............ tonne.
Measurement
4.2
The measurement for winter service duties shall be the quantities ordered by the Service Manager.
Itemisation
4.3
Separate items shall be provided for winter service duties in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4
of GP 112 [Ref 2.N] and the following:
Table 4.3 Itemisation for Winter service duties
Group
Feature
I
1 Operatives for winter service duties.
2 Equipment for winter service duties.
3 Materials for winter service duties.
II
1 Stated resource.
Item Coverage - Operatives for winter service duties
4.4
The items for operatives for winter maintenance duties shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of
GP 113 [Ref 1.N] and include for:
a)
wages and bonus payments;
b)
overtime unless specifically requested by the Service Manager;
c)
National Insurances, levies and taxes;
d)
holiday and statutory contributions;
e)
provision of small tools appropriate to each trade, protective clothing;
f)
travelling time, transport to the site;
g)
welfare facilities;
h)
use of suppliers and subcontractors;
i)
supervision and management.
Item Coverage - Equipment for winter service duties
4.5
The items for equipment for winter service duties shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref
1.N] and include for:
a)
fuel and consumables;
b)
repairs, servicing and maintenance;
c)
insurances and replacement;
d)
transport to site;
e)
use of suppliers and subcontractors;
f)
supervision and management.
