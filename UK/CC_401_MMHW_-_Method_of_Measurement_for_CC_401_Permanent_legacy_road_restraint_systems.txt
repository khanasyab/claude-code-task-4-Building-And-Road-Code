Foreword
Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

9. Re-tensioning of legacy safety barriers
9.
Re-tensioning of legacy safety barriers
Units
9.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
re-tensioning of legacy safety barriers ............ linear metre.
Measurement
9.2
The measurement of re-tensioning of legacy safety barriers shall be the developed length along the
centre line for the containment level class between the following points: the end of each safety barrier
and the interface with connections, transitions and terminals.
9.3
Any re-tensioning undertaken in connection with the repair or replacement of components for legacy
safety barrier, legacy transitions and legacy terminals shall be item coverage under those respective
measurement sections.
9.4
This measurement section shall be used for the specific cyclical maintenance operation of
re-tensioning legacy safety barriers when instructed by the Overseeing Organisation.
Itemisation
9.5
Separate items shall be provided for re-tensioning of legacy safety barrier in accordance with clauses
2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:

Item Coverage - Permanent legacy road restraint systems designed by the Contractor
10.1
The respective coverage rules for permanent legacy road restraint systems above shall be replaced
and/or supplemented with the items below where permanent legacy road restraint systems, or
elements and components thereof, are to be Contractor designed.
10.2
The item coverage for permanent legacy road restraint systems designed by the Contractor, or
elements and components thereof, shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and
include for:
a)
design in accordance with CD 377 [Ref 8.N] and Section 17 of GC 101 [Ref 1.N];
b)
reports and certificates;
c)
provision of data and drawings;
d)
re-submissions and modifications;
e)
amendments to the Works;
f)
obtaining approvals;
g)
everything necessary for the design, fabrication, installation and verification of the Works.
