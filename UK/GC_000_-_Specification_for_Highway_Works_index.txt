Foreword
This index lists the Specification for Highway Works documents, that together with the Work Specific
Requirements, supplementary and alternative clauses, form the contract specification.
Some documents may have country specific requirements. Users are responsible for applying all
appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.
3

Publication summary
Changes introduced in this revision of the index of Specification for Highway Works documents.
4.1
Published
The following documents have been published since the last publication of Specification for Highway
Works documents:
1.
CC 201 Version 1.0.1 - Pavement foundation construction
Release notes:
This document supersedes Series 800 and parts of Series 600,
Series 700, Series 900 and Series 1000, which are withdrawn. It
has been rewritten as part of the MCHW re-write and extensively
restructured. Version 1.0.1 is issued to correct a publication
rendering issue with figure 4.5.
Supersedes:
1. CC 201 Version 1.0.0 - Pavement foundation construction
2. CP 201 Version 1.0.0 - Instructions for specifiers for CC 201
Pavement foundation construction
2.
CC 205 Version 1.0.1 - Maintenance of pavements with an asphalt surfacing
Release notes:
This document supersedes parts of Series 900 and parts of Series
700, Series 800 and Series 1000 which are withdrawn. It has been
re-written as part of the MCHW re-write, extensively restructured
and includes technical revisions. Version 1.0.1 is issued to correct a
publication issue with figure 30.8.
Supersedes:
1. CC 205 Version 1.0.0 - Maintenance of pavements with an
asphalt surfacing
2. CP 205 Version 1.0.0 - Instructions for specifiers for CC 205
Maintenance of pavements with an asphalt surfacing
3.
CC 495 Version 1.0.1 - Miscellaneous
Release notes:
Clauses 2601, 2602, 2603, 2606 and 2608 are re-written and
updated as part of the MCHW re-write. Version 1.0.1 is issued to
correct a publication rendering issue with figure 1.77.
Supersedes:
1. CC 495 Version 1.0.0 - Miscellaneous
2. CP 495 Version 1.0.0 - Instructions for specifiers for CC 495
Miscellaneous
4.
GC 000 31-Oct-2025 - Specification for Highway Works index
Release notes:
Amends and supersedes index dated 30-Sep-2025
Supersedes:
1. GC 000 30-Sep-2025 - Specification for Highway Works index
5.
GC 106 Version 1.0.1 - Working on the highway
Release notes:
Full update and re-write of Series 100 Clauses 117, 118, 119, 120
& 125, as part of the MCHW re-write. Version 1.0.1 published for
the inclusion of contractor proforma for Section 3.
Supersedes:
1. GC 106 Version 1.0.0 - Working on the highway
2. GP 106 Version 1.0.0 - Instructions for specifiers for GC 106
Working on the highway
6.
LC 127 Version 1.0.1 - Fencing
Release notes:
This document replaces Series 300 Fencing as part of the MCHW
re-write. Version 1.0.1 issued to correct a publication issue with the
rendering of figure 6.13.
Supersedes:
1. LC 127 Version 1.0.0 - Fencing
