1. Temporary safety barrier and transitions
1.
Temporary safety barrier and transitions
Units
1.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
supply, install and remove temporary safety barrier ............ linear metre.
b)
maintain temporary safety barrier ............ linear metre per day.
c)
supply, install and remove transition ............ number.
d)
maintain transition ............ day.
Measurement
1.2
The measurement of supply, install and remove temporary safety barrier shall be the developed length
along the centre line of the barrier.
1.3
The measurement of supply, install and remove temporary safety barrier shall be for the complete
installation and removal of the temporary vehicle restraint system.
1.4
The measurement of maintain temporary safety barrier shall be a metre per day for each complete day
during which the temporary safety barrier is supplied and installed.
1.5
The measurement of supply, install and remove temporary transitions shall be for each supplied and
installed.
1.6
The measurement of maintain temporary transitions shall be each complete day during which they are
supplied and installed.
1.7
The measurement of the overall length of supply and install, maintain and remove a temporary safety
barrier shall be the length of need shown on the contract drawings and/or that specified in CC 402 [Ref
5.N].
Note
The actual length over which various VRS reach full containment can vary depending on the system
used.

Temporary vehicle restraint systems designed by the Contractor
Item Coverage - Temporary vehicle restraint systems designed by the Contractor
4.1
The respective coverage rules for temporary vehicle restraint systems above shall be replaced and/or
supplemented with the items below where temporary vehicle restraint systems are to be designed by
the Contractor.
4.2
The items for temporary vehicle restraint systems designed by the Contractor shall be in accordance
with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and include for:
a)
design in accordance with CD 377 [Ref 4.N] and Section 17 of GC 101 [Ref 1.N];

reports and certificates;

re-submissions and modifications;

obtaining approvals;

reinstatement of existing surfaces;
