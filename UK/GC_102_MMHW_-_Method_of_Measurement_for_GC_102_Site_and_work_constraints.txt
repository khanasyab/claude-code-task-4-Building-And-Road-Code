Foreword
Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1.
Control of construction noise and vibration assessment, and
building condition surveys
Units
1.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
assessments and surveys ............ number.
Itemisation
1.2
Separate items shall be provided for control of construction noise and vibration assessments, and
building condition surveys in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 2.N] and the
following:
Table 1.2 Itemisation for Control of construction noise and vibration assessments, and building
condition surveys
Group
Feature
I
1 Control of construction noise and vibration assessment.
2 Building condition survey.
II
1 Different locations.
Item coverage - Control of construction noise and vibration assessments, and building
condition surveys
1.3
The items for control of construction noise and vibration assessments, and building condition surveys
shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 1.N] and include for:
a)
scoping;
b)
screening, consultation and reporting;
c)
develop design objectives;
d)
identify mitigation measures;
e)
provision of a constraints map;
f)
management plans;
g)
method statements;
h)
procedures and records;
i)
preparation of change register;
j)
investigation and monitoring reports;
k)
revisions and updates;
l)
compliance with all relevant requirements as set out in the Design Manual of Roads and Bridges
(DMRB) document LA 111 [Ref 3.N].
