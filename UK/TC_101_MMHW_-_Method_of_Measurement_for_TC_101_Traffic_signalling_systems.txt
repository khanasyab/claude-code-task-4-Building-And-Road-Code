Foreword
Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1. Traffic signal installations
1.
Traffic signal installations
Units
1.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
traffic signal installations ............ item.
Measurement
1.2
The measurement of traffic signal installations shall be the complete installation except for earth
electrodes.
1.3
Earth electrodes shall be measured separately under clauses 8.1, 8.2, 8.2 Note, 8.3, and 8.4 of
TC 501/MMHW [Ref 10.N].
Itemisation
1.4
Separate items shall be provided for traffic signal installations in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and
2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 1.4 Itemisation for Traffic signal installations
Group
Feature
I
1 Traffic signal installations.
II
1 Different locations.
Item Coverage - Traffic signal installations
1.5
The items for traffic signal installations shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and
include for:
a)
excavation of acceptable earthworks material as clauses 3.4 and 3.5 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 8.N];
b)
excavation of unacceptable earthworks material as clause 3.6 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 8.N];
c)
excavation in Hard Material as clause 4.4 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 8.N];
d)
structural concrete as clause 1.4 of CC 482/MMHW [Ref 5.N];
e)
backfilling and compaction;
f)
disposal of unacceptable earthworks material as clause 7.6 of CC 601/MMHW [Ref 8.N];
g)
traffic signal detectors;
h)
detector loops as clause 9.6 of TC 131/MMHW [Ref 9.N];
i)
detector feeders;
j)
outstation cabinets;
k)
trench for cable as clause 5.6 of TC 501/MMHW [Ref 10.N] excluding network cabling;
l)
traffic signal cable and power cable as clause 6.6 of TC 501/MMHW [Ref 10.N] excluding network
cabling;
m)
traffic signal controllers;
n)
photo cell;
o)
control cabinet and base;
p)
auxiliary equipment cabinets and internal equipment;
q)
power supply feeder pillar;

Item Coverage - Traffic signal installations designed by the Contractor
1.6
The respective coverage rules for traffic signalling systems above shall be replaced and/or
supplemented with the items below where traffic signalling systems are to be designed by the
Contractor.
1.7
The item coverage for traffic signalling systems, or aspects thereof designed by the Contractor, shall be
in accordance clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and include for:
a)
design in accordance with TD 101 [Ref 12.N], BS 7671 [Ref 11.N] and section 17 of GC 101 [Ref
1.N];
b)
reports and certificates;
c)
provision of data and drawings;
d)
re-submissions and modifications;
e)
amendments to the Works;
f)
obtaining approvals;
g)
everything necessary for the design, fabrication, installation, verification and commissioning of
the Works.

2. Traffic signal equipment
2.
Traffic signal equipment
Note a
This measurement section is intended for use on scheme renewal contracts where no design is
available for traffic signal installations at the time of tender.
Note b
The itemisation below can include for traffic signal heads, traffic signal posts, detectors, controllers,
cabinets, feeder pillars and chambers etc.
Units
2.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
traffic signal equipment ............ number.
Itemisation
2.2
Separate items shall be provided for traffic signal equipment in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and
2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 2.2 Itemisation for Traffic signal equipment
Group
Feature
I
1 Traffic signal equipment.
II
1 Different types.
III
1 Different sizes.
IV
1 Different locations.
Item Coverage - Traffic signal equipment
2.3
The items for traffic signal equipment shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and
include for:
a)
traffic signal installations as clause 1.5 of this document.
