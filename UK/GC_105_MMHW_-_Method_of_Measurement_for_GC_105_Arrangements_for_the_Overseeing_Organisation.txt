Foreword
Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1. Definitions
1.
Definitions
1.1
"until completion of the works" shall mean until completion of the whole of the works in accordance with
the Conditions of Contract.
1.2
"after completion of the works" shall mean subsequent to clause 1.1 of this document above for the
period stated in the Specification.
1.3
Communication systems shall include for data communications, telephones and radio communication
systems.

2.
Temporary accommodation and equipment for the Overseeing
Organisation
Units
2.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
establishment, and removal of temporary accommodation and equipment ............ item.
b)
servicing of temporary accommodation and equipment ............ item or week.
c)
mobile welfare facilities ............ day or week.
Itemisation
2.2
Separate items shall be provided for temporary accommodation and equipment for the Overseeing
Organisation in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 2.2 Itemisation for Temporary accommodation and equipment for the Overseeing
Organisation
Group
Feature

2 Servicing.
3 Removal.
4 Mobile welfare facilities.
II
1 Temporary accommodation for the Overseeing Organisation.
2 Laboratory and testing equipment for the Overseeing Organisation.
3 Survey equipment for the Overseeing Organisation.
4 Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) for the Overseeing Organisation.
5 Other stated specific equipment for the Overseeing Organisation.
III
1 Provided by the Overseeing Organisation.
IV
1 Offsite accommodation and equipment.
V
1 Until completion of the works.
2 After completion of the works.
Item Coverage - Establishment of temporary accommodation and equipment for the Overseeing
Organisation
2.3
The items for establishment of temporary accommodation and equipment for the Overseeing
Organisation shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and include for:

preparation of sites;

water, sanitation, heating, power and lighting services;
f)
fences, notice and direction boards;
g)
vehicle access, hard standings, parking areas, electric vehicle charging points and footpaths;
h)
equipment, furnishings, fittings, supplies and consumable stores;
i)
display screen equipment,

a)

demolishing and removing off Site temporary accommodation, vehicle access, hard standing,

h)

3. Vehicles for the Overseeing Organisation
3.
Vehicles for the Overseeing Organisation
Units
3.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
vehicles for the Overseeing Organisation ............ vehicle day.
Measurement
3.2
The measurement of vehicles for the Overseeing Organisation shall be each day or part thereof during
which a vehicle is provided.
Itemisation
3.3
Separate items shall be provided for vehicles for the Overseeing Organisation in accordance with
clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 3.3 Itemisation for Vehicles for the Overseeing Organisation
Group
Feature
I
1 Each type of vehicle for the Overseeing Organisation.
II
1 Until completion of the works.
2 After completion of the works.
Item coverage - Vehicles for the Overseeing Organisation
3.4
The items for vehicles for the Overseeing Organisation shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of
GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and include for:
a)
equipment;
b)
taxing for use on public highways and for the carriage of goods and samples;
c)
comprehensive insurance;
d)
suitable replacement including equipment;
e)
depreciation;
f)
maintaining in a roadworthy condition and in conformity with the vehicle manufacturer's
recommendations;
g)
fuel, battery charging for electric vehicles, oil and other consumable items;
h)
keeping clean inside and out;
i)
collecting from Site when the vehicle is returned.

5. Operatives for the Overseeing Organisation
5.
Operatives for the Overseeing Organisation
Units
5.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
operatives for the Overseeing Organisation ............ day or week.
Measurement
5.2
The measurement of operatives for the Overseeing Organisation shall be for the required periods
specified in GC 105 [Ref 1.N].
Note
Shift or nighttime rate adjustments are to be administrated on a project specific basis.
Itemisation
5.3
Separate items shall be provided for operatives for the Overseeing Organisation in accordance with
clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 5.3 Itemisation for Operatives for the Overseeing Organisation
Group
Feature
I
1 Each role of operative for the Overseeing Organisation.
II
1 Until completion of the works.
2 After completion of the works.
Item coverage - Operatives for the Overseeing Organisation
5.4
The items for operatives for the Overseeing Organisation shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of
GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and include for:
a)
the wages and other emoluments paid including payment for overtime;
b)
working outside the Contractor's normal working hours if so required by the Overseeing
Organisation;
c)
costs and expenses incurred consequent upon the employment or hiring;
d)
periods of less than four hours.

6. Progress images
6.
Progress images
Note
Progress images of the works can be captured as stills and/or video.
Units
6.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
set of progress still images, set of aerial progress still images ............ number.
b)
additional progress still images, additional aerial still images ............ number.
c)
set of progress videos, set of aerial progress videos.......... number.
d)
additional progress videos, additional aerial progress videos........... number.
Measurement
6.2
A set of still images or videos shall comprise such numbers of progress images as described in the
Contract taken on any one flight or visit to Site.
6.3
Where in any one flight or visit the Overseeing Organisation orders less than one complete set of
progress images, then one set shall be measured.
6.4
Where in any flight or visit the Overseeing Organisation orders progress images in excess of the
number in the set then the additional progress images shall be measured.
Itemisation
6.5
Separate items shall be provided for progress images in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of
GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 6.5 Itemisation for Progress images
Group
Feature
I
1 Set of progress still images.
2 Set of aerial progress still images.
3 Additional progress still images.
4 Additional aerial progress still images.
5 Set of progress videos.
6 Set of aerial progress videos.
7 Additional progress videos.
8 Additional aerial progress videos.
Item coverage - Progress images
6.6
The items for progress images, aerial progress images, additional progress images and additional
aerial images shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and include for:
a)
identification marking on the progress images;
b)
albums;
c)
video sets;
d)
data transfer media.
