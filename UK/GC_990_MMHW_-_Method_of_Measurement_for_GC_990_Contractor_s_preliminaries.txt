Foreword
Foreword
This document is for incorporation into contracts by reference. It defines coverage of items in Bills of
Quantities and their method of measurement.
This document is applicable to contracts throughout the UK, complemented by the additional method of
measurement, specification requirements and contractual changes of each Overseeing Organisation.
Users are responsible for applying all appropriate documents applicable to their contract.
Users are responsible for archiving contract documentation in accordance with the user's quality
management system.

1.
Temporary accommodation and equipment for the Contractor
Units
1.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
establishment and removal of temporary accommodation and equipment ............ item.
b)
servicing of temporary accommodation and equipment ............ item or week.
c)
mobile welfare facilities ............ day or week.
Itemisation
1.2
Separate items shall be provided for temporary accommodation and equipment for the Contractor in
accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 1.2 Itemisation for Temporary accommodation and equipment for the Contractor
Group
Feature
I
1 Establishment.
2 Servicing.
3 Removal.
4 Mobile welfare facilities.
II
1 Temporary accommodation and equipment for the Contractor.
III
1 At the place of fabrication or manufacture.
2 Offsite.
Item Coverage - Establishment of temporary accommodation and equipment for the Contractor
1.3
The items for establishment of temporary accommodation and equipment for the Contractor shall be in
accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and include for:
a)
everything required by the Contractor, including all temporary accommodation, stores,
workshops, furnishings, fixtures, fitting and equipment;
b)
laboratory spaces and testing equipment;
c)
survey equipment;
d)
consumable items;
e)
personal protective equipment (PPE);
f)
cleaning and waste disposal;
g)
temporary surface water drainage;
h)
temporary protection, and preservation of habitats, trees and shrubs at the location of the
temporary accommodation.
Item Coverage - Servicing of temporary accommodation and equipment for the Contractor
1.4
The items for servicing temporary accommodation for the Contractor shall be in accordance with clause
2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and include for:
a)
establishment of temporary accommodation for the Contractor clause 1.3 of this document.

2. Site equipment for the Contractor
2.
Site equipment for the Contractor
Units
2.1
The unit of measurement shall be:
a)
establishment of each type of site equipment ............ item.
b)
maintenance of each type of site equipment ............ item or week.
c)
removal of each type of site equipment ............ item.
Measurement
2.2
Establishment, maintenance and removal of site equipment for the Contractor shall each be measured
once only for each type of site equipment.
2.3
The measurement of maintenance of site equipment for the Contractor shall be each week or part
thereof during which each type of site equipment is provided.
2.4
The measurement of site equipment for the Contractor shall be for each item within the working area
not included within rates, fee or Section 1 of GC 990/MMHW [Ref 5.N].
Itemisation
2.5
Separate items shall be provided for establishment, maintenance and removal of site equipment in
accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 2.5 Itemisation for Site equipment for the Contractor
Group
Feature
I
1 Establishment.
2 Maintenance.
3 Removal.
II
1 Forklift / telehandler for use by the Contractor.
2 Mobile elevated work platform for use by the Contractor.
3 Overhead power line avoidance including goalposts and height restriction warning for
use by the Contractor.
4 Floodlights for use by the Contractor.
5 Road sweeper for use by the Contractor.
6 Other stated types of site equipment for use by the Contractor.
III
1 At the place of fabrication or manufacture.
2 Offsite.
Item Coverage - Establishment of site equipment for the Contractor
2.6
The items for establishment of site equipment for the Contractor shall be in accordance with clause 2.1
of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and include for:
a)
inspections and submission of certificates;
b)
bringing equipment to site;
c)
establishment of stable footings, foundations, bases, hard standings and shelters;
d)
establishment of all equipment and signs.
Item Coverage - Maintenance of site equipment for the Contractor
2.7
The items for maintenance of site equipment for the Contractor shall be in accordance with clause 2.1
of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and include for:
a)
maintenance of equipment and signs;

3. Project specific management and staff
3.
Project specific management and staff
Units
3.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
project manager, site manager, supervisor, health and safety officer, planner /programmer,
quantity surveyor, engineer, environmental manager, temporary works engineer, storeman,
arboriculturist, public liaison officer, ecologist, traffic management manager ............ hour or day.
Measurement
3.2
The measurement of project specific management and staff shall be each hour or day within the
working area not included in the rates, fee or Section 1 of GC 990/MMHW [Ref 5.N].
Itemisation
3.3
Separate items shall be provided for project specific management and staff in accordance with clauses
2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 3.3 Itemisation for Project specific management and staff
Group
Feature
I
1 Project manager.
2 Site manager.
3 Supervisor (non-operational).
4 Health and safety officer.
5 Planner / programmer.
6 Quantity surveyor.
7 Engineer.
8 Environmental manager.
9 Temporary works engineer.
10 Storeman (non-operational).
11 Arboriculturist.
12 Public liaison officer.
13 Ecologist.
14 Traffic management manager.
15 Other stated resource.
Item coverage - Project specific management and staff
3.4
The items for project specific management and staff shall be in accordance with clause 2.1 of GP 113
[Ref 2.N] and include for wages, salaries, any amounts and payments related to work on the contract,
and those made to staff for:
a)
people costs (as scheduled within the contract);
b)
telephone costs;
c)
broadband or data services;
d)
hybrid working.

4. Temporary works
4.
Temporary works
Units
4.1
The units of measurement shall be:
a)
supply, erect and take down ............ item, lift or square metre.
b)
daily maintenance ............ day.
c)
weekly check ............ week.
Measurement
4.2
The measurement of temporary works including scaffolding, encapsulation, walkways and access
steps shall be for the complete construction.
Itemisation
4.3
Separate items shall be provided for temporary scaffolding, encapsulation, walkways and access steps
in accordance with clauses 2.2, 2.3, and 2.4 of GP 112 [Ref 3.N] and the following:
Table 4.3 Itemisation for Temporary scaffolding, encapsulation, walkways and access steps
Group
Feature
I
1 Supply, erect and take down.
2 Daily maintenance.
3 Weekly check.
II
1 Temporary scaffolding.
2 Temporary encapsulation.
3 Temporary walkway, landings and handrail.
4 Temporary access steps.
5 Other stated temporary works items.
III
1 To abutments, piers and columns.
2 To soffits and beams.
3 To parapet edges.
4 To embankments.
IV
1 Different locations.
Item coverage - Temporary scaffolding, encapsulation, walkways and access steps
4.4
The items for temporary scaffolding, encapsulation, walkways and access steps shall be in accordance
with clause 2.1 of GP 113 [Ref 2.N] and include for:
a)
design, and compliance with Sections 17, 18 and 19 of GC 101 [Ref 1.N];
b)
submission of all temporary works proposals;
c)
reports and certificates;
d)
provision of data and drawings;
e)
re-submissions and modifications;
f)
amendments to the Works;
g)
obtaining technical approvals;
h)
everything necessary for the design, fabrication, installation and testing of the Works.
