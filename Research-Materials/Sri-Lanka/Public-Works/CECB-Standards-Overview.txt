================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

The Central Engineering Consultancy Bureau (CECB) was established in 1973 as
a fully owned State Enterprise by the Government of Sri Lanka. It operates as
a self-financed Government Corporation and is primarily involved in providing
engineering consultancy, construction, and related services across multiple
infrastructure sectors.

OWNERSHIP: 100% Government of Sri Lanka
OPERATIONAL MODEL: Self-financing
NATURE: State-owned consultancy and construction services provider
