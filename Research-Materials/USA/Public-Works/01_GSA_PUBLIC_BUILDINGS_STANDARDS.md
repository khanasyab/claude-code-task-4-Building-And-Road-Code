# GSA PUBLIC BUILDINGS STANDARDS AND DESIGN GUIDES

## Overview
The General Services Administration (GSA) is responsible for the design, construction, management, and preservation of federal government buildings. GSA publishes comprehensive facilities standards and design guides that establish requirements for federal buildings and serve as models for public buildings nationwide.

## Official Source
- **Agency**: General Services Administration (GSA)
- **Division**: Public Buildings Service (PBS)
- **Website**: https://www.gsa.gov
- **Standards Website**: https://www.gsa.gov/real-estate/design-construction
- **Headquarters**: Washington, D.C.
