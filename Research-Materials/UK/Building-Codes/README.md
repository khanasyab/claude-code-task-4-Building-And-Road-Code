## Complementary Standards

UK Building Regulations work alongside:
- British Standards (BS)
- Eurocodes (BS EN 1990-1999)
- Guidance from CIBSE, CIRIA, BRE

## Notes

- Approved Documents show compliance methods but are not mandatory
