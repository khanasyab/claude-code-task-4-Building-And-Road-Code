# UK ROAD STANDARDS

This directory contains comprehensive research on UK highway design and construction standards.

## Contents

### Main Documents

1. **UK-DMRB-Design-Manual-Roads-Bridges-Complete.txt**
   - Complete reference to the Design Manual for Roads and Bridges (DMRB)
   - Official standards for trunk roads and motorways
   - Published by National Highways
   - Includes Manual of Contract Documents for Highway Works (MCHW)

## Coverage

### DMRB Document Series Covered:

- **GG Series**: General Governance (including GG 000 master index)
- **GD Series**: Geometric Design (junctions, roundabouts, speed limits)
- **CD Series**: Structural Design (bridges, culverts, retaining walls, tunnels)
- **CS Series**: Structural Assessment (inspection, assessment of existing structures)
- **LD Series**: Environmental Design (landscape, biodiversity, mitigation)
- **LA Series**: Environmental Assessment (all environmental topics)
- **TD Series**: Traffic Signs and Road Markings

### Manual of Contract Documents for Highway Works (MCHW):

- **Volume 1**: Specification for Highway Works (all series 000-2200)
- **Volume 2**: Notes for Guidance
- **Volume 3**: Highway Construction Details
- **Volume 4**: Bills of Quantities
- **Volume 5**: Framework Contracts
