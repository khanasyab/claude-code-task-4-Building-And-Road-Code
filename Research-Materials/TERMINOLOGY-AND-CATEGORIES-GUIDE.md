# Building & Roads Code - Complete Categories and Terminology Guide
## What to Download and What Terms to Search For

**Purpose:** This guide explains all the categories you need and the correct terminology to use when searching for codes.

---

## Part 1: BUILDING CODES Categories

### 1. General Building Construction
**Search Terms:**
- "Building Code" or "International Building Code (IBC)"
- "Construction standards"
- "Structural requirements"

**What This Covers:**
- Commercial buildings (offices, shops, factories)
- Multi-family residential (apartments)
- Mixed-use buildings
- High-rise buildings
- Building foundations and structure
- Load-bearing requirements

**USA Sources:**
- IBC (International Building Code): https://up.codes/code/international-building-code-ibc-2024
- IBC covers Chapters 1-35 including: Scope, Occupancy, Types of Construction, Heights & Areas, Fire Ratings, Structural Design, Materials, etc.

## Part 2: ROAD AND HIGHWAY CODES Categories

### 1. Road Design and Geometry
**Search Terms:**
- "Highway Design Standards" or "Geometric Design"
- "Road design manual"
- "AASHTO Green Book"

**What This Covers:**
- Road alignment (horizontal and vertical curves)
- Lane widths
- Shoulder requirements
- Sight distances
- Intersection design
- Superelevation (banking of curves)
- Cross slopes and drainage

**USA Sources:**
- AASHTO Green Book (Policy on Geometric Design of Highways and Streets)
- Purchase from: https://store.transportation.org/
- FHWA Highway Design Handbook: https://highways.dot.gov/

**UK Sources:**
- DMRB (Design Manual for Roads and Bridges)
- CD 109: Highway link design
- CD 116: Geometric design of roundabouts
- Access: https://www.standardsforhighways.co.uk/

---

### 2. Pavement Design

**India Sources:**

## IMPORTANT NOTES

### USA Codes
- **All I-Codes are FREE online** at UpCodes and ICC
- **Cannot download PDFs** (copyrighted) but can view online
- **Can copy/paste from UpCodes** for research

### UK Codes
- **Building Regulations Approved Documents** - FREE PDFs from gov.uk
- **DMRB (Roads)** - FREE from standardsforhighways.co.uk
- **Currently have 403 access issues** - may need manual download

### India Codes
- **IRC codes** - Roads and highways
- **NBC** - National Building Code
- **Some FREE, some require purchase** from BIS or IRC
