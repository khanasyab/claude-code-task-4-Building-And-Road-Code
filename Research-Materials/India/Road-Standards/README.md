# Indian Road Standards and Specifications - Research Documentation

## Overview
This folder contains research and documentation on Indian Road Congress (IRC) standards, specifications, and codes of practice for road and highway construction.

---

## 1. INDIAN ROADS CONGRESS (IRC)

### Organization Details
- **Full Name:** Indian Roads Congress
- **Establishment:** Government of India
- **Type:** Apex Body of Highway Engineers in India
- **Parent Ministry:** Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MoRTH)
- **Website:** https://www.irc.nic.in/
- **Headquarters:** Jamnagar House, Shahjahan Road, New Delhi

### Mission
The IRC works in close collaboration with MoRTH to develop standards, specifications, codes of practices, and manuals for all aspects of highway engineering including roads, bridges, tunnels, and traffic management.

### Total Publications
- **275+ Standards** covering:
  - Roads
  - Bridges
  - Tunnels
  - Traffic Management
  - Highway Engineering
  - Specifications
  - Codes of Practice
  - Design Manuals
  - Guidelines

## 2. MAJOR IRC STANDARDS AND RECENT 2024-2025 PUBLICATIONS

### IRC:78-2024 (Part-I) - Foundation and Substructure
**Title:** Standard Specifications and Code of Practice for Road Bridges, Foundations and Substructure Part I General Features and Working Stress Design
- **Edition:** Third Revision
- **Released:** 2024
- **Coverage:**
  - Foundation design for road bridges
  - Substructure specifications
  - General features and requirements
  - Working stress design methodology

### IRC:75-2025 (Part-2) - Flood Protection
**Title:** Guidelines for Retrofitting Measures for Protection of Road Systems Including Surrounding Areas From Rainfall, Waterlogging and Flooding
- **Edition:** Part 2
- **Released:** 2025
- **Coverage:**
  - Retrofitting measures
  - Rainfall protection
  - Waterlogging prevention
  - Flood protection systems
  - Road system resilience
