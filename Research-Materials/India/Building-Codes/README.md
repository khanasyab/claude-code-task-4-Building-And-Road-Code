## 1. NATIONAL BUILDING CODE OF INDIA (NBC) 2016

### Official Source
- **Publisher:** Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS)
- **Standard Code:** SP 7:2016
- **Website:** https://www.bis.gov.in/standards/technical-department/national-building-code/
- **Latest Version:** NBC 2016 (Released 2016, No 2024 version exists)
- **Format:** 2-Volume Set

### Structure
NBC 2016 is organized into **12 Parts** divided into **33 Chapters**:

#### Part 1: Administration
- Chapter 1: Definitions, Symbols and Abbreviations
- Chapter 2: General Building Requirements

#### Part 2: Development Control Rules and General Building Requirements

#### Part 3: Fire and Life Safety
- Fire prevention and protection
- Means of egress
- Fire protection systems
- Building classification by occupancy
- Fire resistance requirements

#### Part 4: Fire and Life Safety
- Comprehensive fire safety provisions
- Active and passive fire protection
- Emergency evacuation requirements
- Fire detection and suppression systems

#### Part 5: Building Materials
- Specifications for construction materials
- Material quality standards
- Testing requirements

#### Part 6: Structural Design
- Loads on buildings
- Foundation design
- Structural design requirements

#### Part 7: Constructional Practices
- Construction methods and standards
- Quality control during construction

#### Part 8: Building Services
- Plumbing services
- Water supply
- Drainage and sanitation
- Electrical installations
- Lifts and escalators
- HVAC systems

#### Part 9: Plumbing Services
- Detailed plumbing specifications
- Water supply and drainage
- Sanitary fixtures

#### Part 10: Approach to Sustainability
- Green building practices
- Energy efficiency
- Sustainable construction

#### Part 11: Approach to Improving Resilience of Buildings and Infrastructure Against Natural Hazards
- Earthquake resistance
- Wind load considerations
- Flood protection

#### Part 12: Earthquake Resistant Design and Construction of Buildings

### Key Features
- Incorporates international best practices
- Addresses natural disaster resilience
- Model code for adoption by states and local authorities
- Updated from 2005 version with amendments through 2015
- Comprehensive coverage of all building construction aspects

### Historical Versions
- First version: 1970
- Second revision: 1983
- Third revision: 2005 (with amendments in 2015)
- Fourth revision: 2016 (Current)

### Availability
- **Official Purchase:** BIS website and offices
- **Internet Archive:** Free download available
  - Volume 1: https://archive.org/details/nationalbuilding01
  - Volume 2: https://archive.org/details/nationalbuilding02
- **Guide:** Booklet-Guide-for-Using-NBC-2016.pdf available on BIS website

## 2. MODEL BUILDING BYE-LAWS 2016

### Official Source
- **Publisher:** Town and Country Planning Organisation (TCPO)
- **Ministry:** Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, Government of India
- **Released:** March 18, 2016
- **Website:** http://tcpo.gov.in/model-building-bye-laws-mbbl-2016

### Purpose
Legal tools to regulate:
- Building coverage
- Building height
- Building bulk and density
- Architectural design
- Construction aspects
- Orderly urban development

### Key Updates from 2004 Version
1. **Environmental Provisions:**
   - Norms for Rooftop Solar PV Installation
   - Green building requirements
   - Energy efficiency standards

2. **Safety and Security:**
   - Enhanced structural safety requirements
   - Fire safety provisions
   - Natural hazard protection (earthquake, flood)

3. **Social Infrastructure:**
   - Segregated sanitation facilities in public buildings
   - Barrier-free environment for disabled, children, and elderly
   - Universal accessibility requirements

4. **Heritage Conservation:**
   - Conservation of heritage buildings
   - Heritage precincts protection
   - Natural feature areas preservation

5. **Modern Standards:**
   - Bye-laws for safe use of glass
   - Electromagnetic radiation mitigation
   - Digital infrastructure provisions

6. **Swachh Bharat Mission Integration:**
   - Sanitation standards
   - Waste management requirements

### Application
- Guidance for State Governments
- Urban Local Bodies adoption
- Urban Development Authorities implementation
- Mandatory nature for regulated development

## 3. BIS INDIAN STANDARDS (IS CODES) FOR BUILDINGS

### Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS)
- **Establishment:** Reconstituted under BIS Act 2016 (effective October 12, 2017)
- **Parent Ministry:** Department of Consumer Affairs
- **Total Standards:** Over 20,000 IS codes across all sectors
- **Website:** https://www.bis.gov.in

### Major IS Codes for Building Construction

#### IS 456:2000 - Plain and Reinforced Concrete
- **Status:** Reaffirmed 2021
- **Latest Amendment:** Amendment No. 6 (April 2024)
- **Design Approach:** Limit State Design (recommended), Working Stress Design
- **Coverage:**
  - Concrete mix design
  - Material specifications
  - Reinforcement detailing
  - Structural design
  - Construction practices
  - Durability requirements

**April 2024 Amendment Updates:**
- Core/cube tests for concrete strength estimation
- Fusion bonded epoxy coated reinforcing bars
- Mineral admixtures (fly ash and GGBS)
- Limits on cementitious material content

#### IS 800:2007 - General Construction in Steel
- **Status:** Current (Third Revision)
- **Design Approach:** Limit State Method
- **Coverage:**
  - Steel structures design
  - Frames, trusses, beams, columns
  - Load combinations and safety factors
  - Bolted connections
  - Welded connections
  - Riveted connections
  - Buckling analysis
  - Connection design

#### IS 1893 - Earthquake Resistant Design
- **Part 1:2016** - General Provisions and Buildings (Reaffirmed 2021)
- **Part 2:2024** - Seventh Revision (Adopted November 2024)
- **Part 4:2024** - Industrial Structures Including Stack-Like Structures (Second Revision)

**Coverage:**
- Seismic zone mapping (5 zones)
- Design spectrum
- Earthquake forces calculation
- Structural response
- Detailing requirements
- Industrial structures provisions

**2024 Updates:**
- Updated definitions
- Revised design spectrum
- Enhanced industrial structure provisions
- Improved precision and safety

#### IS 2062 - Steel for General Structural Purposes
- Hot rolled structural steel specifications
- Grades and quality requirements
- Testing standards

#### IS 1786 - High Strength Deformed Steel Bars
- Reinforcing steel specifications
- Strength requirements
- Quality standards

#### IS 875 - Code of Practice for Design Loads
- Part 1: Dead Loads
- Part 2: Imposed Loads (Live Loads)
- Part 3: Wind Loads
- Part 4: Snow Loads
- Part 5: Special Loads and Load Combinations

### ISI Mark Certification
- Quality certification mark
- Indicates compliance with IS standards
- Rigorous testing required
- Available for building materials meeting BIS requirements

### Accessing IS Codes
1. **Purchase:** BIS website or physical offices
2. **E-Library:** BIS subscriber portal
3. **Free Access:** Indigenous Indian Standards available free of cost
4. **Format:** Print and digital formats available

## 4. FIRE SAFETY STANDARDS

### National Building Code Part 4 - Fire and Life Safety
Comprehensive fire safety provisions integrated into NBC 2016 Part 4

**Coverage:**
- Fire Prevention
- Fire Detection and Alarm Systems
- Firefighting Equipment
- Means of Egress Standards
- Exit and stairway requirements
- Emergency evacuation routes
- Fire Protection Systems
- Sprinkler systems
- Fire extinguishers
- Smoke control systems
- Building Classification by occupancy
- Fire Resistance Requirements based on:
  - Building height
  - Occupancy type
  - Building use
- Passive Fire Protection
- Fire barriers
- Fire doors
- Fire dampers
- Compartmentation
