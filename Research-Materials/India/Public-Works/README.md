# Indian Public Works Department - Research Documentation

## Overview
This folder contains research and documentation on Central Public Works Department (CPWD) specifications, manuals, and State PWD codes across India.

---

## 1. CENTRAL PUBLIC WORKS DEPARTMENT (CPWD)

### Organization Details
- **Full Name:** Central Public Works Department
- **Parent Ministry:** Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, Government of India
- **Role:** Central government's principal civil engineering construction agency
- **Website:** https://cpwd.gov.in/
- **Jurisdiction:** Central government construction projects across India

### Mandate
CPWD is responsible for:
- Construction and maintenance of central government buildings
- Design and execution of civil engineering projects
- Development of construction standards and specifications
- Quality assurance in public works
- Project management for government infrastructure

### UTTAR PRADESH PWD

**Organization:** Public Works Department, Uttar Pradesh
**Website:** uppwd.gov.in

**Key Documents:**
- UP PWD Manual
- UP PWD Rules
- UP PWD Specifications
- Schedule of Rates

**Document Categories:**

**Specifications (U.P.P.W.D Specification):**
Building Classifications:
- **Class A Buildings:** High-end (Circuit Houses, Medical Colleges)
  - Materials: Concrete, RCC, aluminum, stainless steel
  - Highest specifications

- **Class B Buildings:** Medium grade (Inter-colleges, Hospitals)
  - Materials: Pressed steel, zinc
  - Moderate specifications

- **Class C Buildings:** Basic (Primary schools, Junior High Schools)
  - Materials: Angle iron, commercial shutters
  - Standard specifications

**Available Documents:**
- PWD Rules (1.PWD-RULES.pdf)
- Specifications for Buildings (SpecBuildGon.pdf)
- Guidelines for Road Works
- Model Bidding Documents (T-1 for works up to Rs. 40 Lacs)
- Schedule of Rates (Rate List section)

**Manual of Orders:**
- Volume I, 3rd Edition
- Para 507: Register of Contractors (confidential record)
- Maintained at Divisional and Sub-divisional offices
