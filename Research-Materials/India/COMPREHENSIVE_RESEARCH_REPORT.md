## I. NATIONAL FRAMEWORK DOCUMENTS

### 1. NATIONAL BUILDING CODE OF INDIA (NBC) 2016

**Official Details:**
- **Standard Code:** SP 7:2016
- **Publisher:** Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS)
- **Version:** NBC 2016 (Latest - no 2024 version exists)
- **Format:** 2-Volume Set
- **Structure:** 12 Parts, 33 Chapters

**Key Features:**
- Comprehensive building construction guidelines
- Fire and life safety (Part 4)
- Structural design standards (Part 6)
- Building services (Part 8, 9)
- Sustainability provisions (Part 10)
- Natural hazard resilience (Part 11, 12)

**Access:**
- Official: BIS website (bis.gov.in)
- Free: Internet Archive (archive.org)
  - Volume 1: /details/nationalbuilding01
  - Volume 2: /details/nationalbuilding02
- Guide: Booklet-Guide-for-Using-NBC-2016.pdf

**Status:** Current national standard, adopted 2016

**Schedule of 39+ project classes including:**

- Highways and infrastructure
- Industrial projects
- Mining operations

### EIA Process
1. **Screening** - Categorization (A or B)
2. **Scoping** - Terms of Reference
3. **EIA Study** - Baseline data, impact assessment, mitigation
4. **Public Consultation** - Public hearing, stakeholder feedback
5. **Appraisal** - Expert Committee review
6. **Clearance** - Grant with conditions
