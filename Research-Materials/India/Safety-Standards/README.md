# Indian Safety and Technical Standards - Research Documentation

## Overview
This folder contains research and documentation on Indian safety standards, electrical installation codes, water supply regulations, and environmental clearance requirements.

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL CLEARANCE AND SAFETY

### Environment Impact Assessment (EIA) Notification, 2006

**Authority:** Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEF&CC)
**Effective:** September 14, 2006 (with subsequent amendments)

#### EIA Amendment Notification 2024
**Key Changes:**
- Mandatory digital submission via PARIVESH 2.0 portal
- Streamlined approval process
- Enhanced monitoring mechanisms
- Transparency measures

### Projects Requiring Environmental Clearance

**Schedule Classification:**
Over 39 classes of projects requiring prior EC, including:

#### Building and Construction Projects
- Area development projects
- Building construction with built-up area up to 1,50,000 sq.m
- Township projects
- Commercial complexes

#### Infrastructure Projects
- Highways
- Ports and harbors
- Airports
- Railway lines

#### Industrial Projects
- Thermal power plants
- Mining operations
- Chemical industries
- Cement plants

### EIA Process

#### Screening
- Project categorization (A or B)
- Requirement determination
- Initial review

#### Scoping
- Terms of Reference (TOR)
- Study area definition
- Assessment parameters

#### EIA Study
- Baseline data collection
- Impact prediction
- Mitigation measures
- Environmental Management Plan (EMP)

#### Public Consultation
- Public hearing
- Stakeholder feedback
- Response to concerns

#### Appraisal
- Expert Appraisal Committee (EAC) review
- Technical evaluation
- Recommendation

#### Clearance
- Grant of Environmental Clearance
- Conditions and safeguards
- Validity period

### PARIVESH 2.0 Portal
**Website:** parivesh.nic.in
**Features:**
- Single-window clearance system
- Online application submission
- Status tracking
- Document management
- Integrated approvals:
  - Environment Clearance
  - Forest Clearance
  - Wildlife Clearance
  - Coastal Regulation Zone clearance

### Compliance and Monitoring
- Half-yearly compliance reports
- Online submission
- Third-party monitoring
- Penalties for non-compliance
