# India - What to Download
## Complete Guide to Indian Building and Road Codes

**Quick Answer:** India has **3 main sources** (not just CPWD):
1. **NBC** (National Building Code) - For BUILDINGS
2. **IRC** (Indian Roads Congress) - For ROADS/HIGHWAYS
3. **CPWD** (Central Public Works Department) - For GOVERNMENT CONSTRUCTION PROCEDURES

---

## 1. NBC - National Building Code of India 2016
### (Like USA's IBC - For ALL Buildings)

**What it is:**
- India's main building code
- Covers ALL types of buildings (residential, commercial, industrial)
- 2-volume set with 12 parts and 33 chapters

**What it covers:**
- Building design and construction
- Fire and life safety
- Structural requirements
- Plumbing and drainage
- Electrical installations
- HVAC systems
- Accessibility standards
- Earthquake/seismic design
- Sustainability

**Where to download:**
- **Official Purchase:** https://www.services.bis.gov.in/ (search "SP 7:2016")
- **Free Access (Internet Archive):**
  - Volume 1: https://archive.org/details/nationalbuilding01
  - Volume 2: https://archive.org/details/nationalbuilding02
- **Guide:** Search "Booklet-Guide-for-Using-NBC-2016.pdf"

**Standard Code:** SP 7:2016
**Published by:** Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS)
**Version:** NBC 2016 (Latest - still current in 2025)

---

## 2. IRC - Indian Roads Congress Standards
### (For ROADS, HIGHWAYS, BRIDGES)

**What it is:**
- India's road and highway design standards
- 275+ publications covering all aspects of roads
- Published by Indian Roads Congress under Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

**Key IRC Standards:**

### Road Design:
- **IRC:73** - Geometric Design Standards for Rural Highways
- **IRC:86** - Geometric Design Standards for Urban Roads
- **IRC:SP:84** - Manual of Specifications & Standards for Four Laning

### Pavement Design:
- **IRC:37** - Guidelines for Design of Flexible Pavements (Asphalt)
- **IRC:58** - Guidelines for Design of Rigid Pavements (Concrete)
- **IRC:SP:62** - Guidelines for Design and Construction of Cement Concrete Pavements

### Bridge Design:
- **IRC:5** - Standard Specifications for Road Bridges - Section I (General Features)
- **IRC:6** - Standard Specifications for Road Bridges - Section II (Loads & Stresses)
- **IRC:21** - Standard Specifications for Road Bridges - Section III (Cement Concrete)
- **IRC:24** - Standard Specifications for Road Bridges - Section V (Steel Road Bridges)
- **IRC:78** - Standard Specifications for Road Bridges - Section VII (Foundations)
- **IRC:112** - Code of Practice for Concrete Road Bridges

### Traffic Engineering:
- **IRC:35** - Code of Practice for Road Markings
- **IRC:67** - Code of Practice for Road Signs
- **IRC:93** - Guidelines on Design and Installation of Road Traffic Signals
- **IRC:106** - Guidelines for Capacity of Urban Roads in Plain Areas

### Drainage:
- **IRC:SP:13** - Guidelines for the Design of Small Bridges and Culverts
- **IRC:SP:42** - Guidelines on Road Drainage

### Materials & Construction:
- **IRC:29** - Standard Specifications for Bituminous Construction
- **IRC:44** - Guidelines for Cement Concrete Mix Design for Pavements
- **IRC:15** - Standard Specifications and Code of Practice for Construction

### Safety:
- **IRC:SP:87** - Manual of Specifications and Standards for Two Laning of State Highways (includes traffic safety)
- **IRC:99** - Tentative Guidelines on the Provision of Speed Breakers

**Where to download:**
- **Official:** https://irc.nic.in/ or https://irc.org.in/
- **Purchase:** Many require purchase from IRC
- **Free versions:** Some available through government libraries or educational institutions
- **Check:** Your research folder already has comprehensive list of all 275+ IRC publications

**Note:** IRC standards are similar to AASHTO standards in USA

## 3. CPWD - Central Public Works Department
### (For GOVERNMENT CONSTRUCTION PROCEDURES & SPECIFICATIONS)

**What it is:**
- Central government's construction agency
- Provides specifications, procedures, and contract formats
- Used primarily for government projects

**Key CPWD Documents:**

### A. CPWD Works Manual 2024
**What it covers:**
- Establishment rules and procedures
- Works planning and execution
- Maintenance guidelines
- Financial procedures
- Contract management
- Quality assurance

**Where to download:**
- Official: https://cpwd.gov.in/
- Free download from CPWD Publications section

### B. CPWD Specifications 2019
**What it covers:**
Complete construction specifications for:
- Earthwork and excavation
- Concrete works
- Masonry works
- Steel works
- Carpentry and joinery
- Painting and finishing
- Flooring
- Roofing
- Doors and windows
- Electrical installations
- Plumbing and sanitary
- HVAC works
- Fire protection systems

**Where to download:**
- https://cpwd.gov.in/Publication/Specifications.aspx
- Free PDF download available

### C. General Conditions of Contract (GCC)
- **GCC 2023** - Latest version for construction contracts
- **GCC 2019** - Previous version (still widely used)

**Contract Forms:**
- CPWD Form 6: Tender submission
- CPWD Form 7: Contract clauses
- CPWD Form 8: Agreement execution
- CPWD Form 12: Supplementary documents

**Where to download:**
- https://cpwd.gov.in/Publication/contracts.aspx
- Free download

### D. CPWD Analysis of Rates
- Standard rates for construction items
- Material specifications
- Labor requirements
- Equipment usage rates

**Where to download:**
- https://cpwd.gov.in/Publication/DARReports.aspx

### E. Delhi Schedule of Rates (DSR)
- Current rates for Delhi region
- Used as reference nationwide
- Updated annually

## 4. BIS - Bureau of Indian Standards
### (Specific Technical Standards)

**What it is:**
- India's national standards body (like ANSI in USA)
- 20,000+ standards across all industries
- 500+ standards related to construction

**Key BIS Standards for Construction:**

### Structural Design:
- **IS 456:2000** - Plain and Reinforced Concrete (Reaffirmed 2021)
- **IS 800:2007** - General Construction in Steel
- **IS 13920:2016** - Ductile Detailing of RC Structures

### Earthquake Design:
- **IS 1893 (Part 1):2016** - Earthquake Resistant Design of Structures (Buildings)
- **IS 1893 (Part 2):2024** - Liquid Retaining Tanks
- **IS 1893 (Part 4):2024** - Industrial Structures

### Design Loads:
- **IS 875 (Part 1)** - Dead Loads
- **IS 875 (Part 2)** - Imposed Loads (Live Loads)
- **IS 875 (Part 3)** - Wind Loads
- **IS 875 (Part 4)** - Snow Loads
- **IS 875 (Part 5)** - Special Loads and Load Combinations

### Materials:
- **IS 2062** - Steel for Structural Purposes
- **IS 1786** - High Strength Deformed Steel Bars
- **IS 269** - Ordinary Portland Cement
- **IS 383** - Coarse and Fine Aggregate for Concrete

### Water & Sanitation:
- **IS 10500** - Drinking Water Quality Specifications
- **IS 3025** - Water Testing Methods (58 parts)

**Where to download:**
- **Official Purchase:** https://www.services.bis.gov.in/
- **Free Access:** Some available through educational institutions
- **Note:** Most require purchase from BIS

## 5. Other Important Indian Sources

### A. General Financial Rules (GFR) 2017
- Ministry of Finance
- Public procurement procedures
- Government contracts and bidding
- Free download: https://doe.gov.in/gfr-2017

### B. Manual for Procurement of Works (2022)
- Detailed procurement procedures
- Tendering process
- Contract types
- Free download from Ministry of Finance website

### C. CPHEEO Manuals (Water & Sanitation)
- Manual on Water Supply and Treatment
- Manual on Sewerage and Sewage Treatment
- Published by Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs
- Free download from CPHEEO website

### D. State PWD Codes
Each state has Public Works Department with local codes:
- Maharashtra PWD
- Tamil Nadu PWD
- Karnataka PWD
- Uttar Pradesh PWD
- Rajasthan PWD
- West Bengal PWD

**Note:** Often based on CPWD/NBC with state modifications
