# Indian Technical Specifications - Research Documentation

## Overview
This folder contains consolidated information on technical specifications for construction works, materials, and systems used in Indian public works projects.

## 1. CPWD TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Overview
CPWD (Central Public Works Department) develops and maintains comprehensive technical specifications for all types of construction works undertaken by the central government.

### Categories of Specifications

**Latest Updates:** Continuously revised
**Coverage:** Complete specifications for building and infrastructure works

**Major Categories:**

- Depth classifications
- Soil classification
- Backfilling procedures

- **IS 8112:** OPC 43 Grade
- **IS 12269:** OPC 53 Grade
- **IS 1489:** Portland Pozzolana Cement (PPC)
- **IS 455:** Portland Slag Cement
- **IS 12600:** Low Heat Portland Cement

**Quality Requirements:**
- Fineness
- Setting time
- Soundness
- Compressive strength
- Chemical composition

### Aggregates

**Coarse Aggregates:**
- **IS 383:** Specifications
- Size: 20mm, 12mm, 10mm nominal
- Shape: Angular, crushed
- Quality tests: Crushing value, impact value, abrasion value

**Fine Aggregates:**
- **IS 383:** Specifications
- Grading zones (I, II, III, IV)
- Silt content limits
- Quality requirements

**Tests:**
