## Key Findings Summary

### Primary Codes and Standards

#### 1. Bangladesh National Building Code (BNBC) 2020
- **Status:** Mandatory national building code
- **Published:** Gazetted February 2021, Released February 18, 2022
- **Publisher:** Housing and Building Research Institute (HBRI)
- **Ministry:** Housing and Public Works
- **Technical Basis:** Based on ASCE 7-05 with adaptations
- **Structure:** 3 Volumes, 5 Parts
- **Coverage:** Structural design, fire safety, MEP systems, materials
- **Previous Versions:** BNBC 1993, BNBC 2006, BNBC 2012 (partial)

**Key Chapters:**
- Chapter 5: Maintenance, Repairs, Retrofitting, Strengthening
- Chapter 8: Detailing of Reinforcement in Concrete
- Chapter 13: Steel-Concrete Composite Members
- Seismic design provisions
- Fire safety requirements
- Building services (electrical, plumbing, HVAC)

#### 2. PWD Schedule of Rates 2022 (Revised)
- **Effective Date:** February 23, 2023
- **Publisher:** Public Works Department, Ministry of Housing and Public Works
- **Type:** Part A - Civil Works
- **Purpose:** Standard rates and specifications for government projects
- **Basis:** Zero lead rates (excluding transportation costs)

**Coverage:**
- General requirements and safety
- Excavation and earthwork
- Concrete works and foundations
- Brick and masonry works
- Finishing works
- All construction activities

#### 3. Roads and Highways Department (RHD) Standards
- **Organization:** Ministry of Road Transport and Bridges
- **Scope:** National highways, regional roads, bridges
- **Standards:** Design, construction, and maintenance specifications
- **Alignment:** AASHTO, IRC (India), British Standards, ASTM

**Key Areas:**
- Geometric design standards
- Pavement engineering
- Bridge design and construction
- Materials specifications
- Quality control procedures

#### 4. Housing and Building Research Institute (HBRI)
- **Established:** 2016 (through Act 2016)
- **Ministry:** Housing and Public Works
- **Role:** Building code development, research, testing

**Activities:**
- BNBC development and maintenance
- Construction technology research
- Disaster-resistant housing
- Alternative building materials
- Ferrocement technology
- Testing and certification

#### 5. Department of Public Health Engineering (DPHE)
- **Ministry:** Local Government Division
- **Scope:** Water supply and sanitation (rural and small towns)
- **Standards:** Water quality, supply systems, sanitation

**Key Standards:**
- Water Quality Parameters (Bangladesh Standards + WHO Guidelines)
- Arsenic: 0.05 mg/L (Bangladesh standard)
- E. coli: Zero/100 mL sample
- Design and construction standards for water supply
- Sanitation facility standards

## Comparison to Pakistan Building & Roads Code

### Similarities:

1. **Historical Framework:**
   - Both evolved from pre-1947 British colonial PWD system
   - Similar administrative structures
   - Common legal heritage (common law)
   - Comparable organizational frameworks

2. **Institutional Structure:**
   - Public Works Department (PWD) organization
   - Roads department for highways
   - Ministry-level oversight
   - Similar approval and enforcement mechanisms

3. **Technical Approaches:**
   - Schedule of rates for government projects
   - Specification-based standards
   - Mix of British and American standards
   - Similar construction practices

4. **Coverage Areas:**
   - Building construction
   - Road and bridge infrastructure
   - Water supply and sanitation
   - Public works projects

### Differences:

1. **Code Modernization:**
   - Bangladesh: BNBC 2020 (recently updated, modern code)
   - Pakistan: B&R Code 1989/1994 (older framework)

2. **International Alignment:**
   - Bangladesh: ASCE 7-05 basis, modern international codes
   - Pakistan: Mix of British and older international standards

3. **Organizational Evolution:**
   - Bangladesh: Specialized agencies (RHD, HBRI, DPHE)
   - Pakistan: More traditional PWD structure

4. **Seismic Design:**
   - Bangladesh: Modern seismic provisions in BNBC 2020
   - Pakistan: Seismic considerations but older framework
