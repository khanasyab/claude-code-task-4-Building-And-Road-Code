================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

The Housing and Building Research Institute (HBRI) is the premier research
organization in Bangladesh for housing and building construction. It serves as
the national authority for building code development, construction research,
and technical guidance in the building sector.

MISSION:
- Research and development in building construction
- Development of cost-effective building technologies
- Technical support for housing sector
- Building code development and maintenance
- Training and capacity building
