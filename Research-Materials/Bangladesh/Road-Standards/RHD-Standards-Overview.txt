================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

The Roads and Highways Department (RHD) is the principal government agency
responsible for planning, design, construction, and maintenance of the national
highway and road network in Bangladesh. RHD develops and maintains comprehensive
standards and specifications for road construction and infrastructure projects.

JURISDICTION:
- National highways
- Regional highways
- Important district roads
- Major bridges and structures

================================================================================

================================================================================
REGIONAL COMPARISON
================================================================================

SIMILARITIES TO PAKISTAN:
- Both countries inherited PWD and road department structures from pre-1947
- Similar organizational frameworks
- Comparable road classification systems
- Similar design standards adapted from British and AASHTO standards

SOUTH ASIAN CONTEXT:
- Coordination with SAARC (South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation)
- Regional highway connectivity projects
- Shared technical challenges (flooding, cyclones, seismic activity)
- Common design approaches
