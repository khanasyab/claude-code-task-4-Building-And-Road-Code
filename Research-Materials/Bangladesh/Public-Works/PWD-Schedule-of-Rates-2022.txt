================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

The PWD Schedule of Rates (SoR) 2022 is a comprehensive pricing guide and
specification document for civil works undertaken by the Public Works Department
and other government agencies in Bangladesh. It provides standardized rates,
specifications, and methodologies for construction projects.

TYPE: Part A - Civil Works
STATUS: Official government document, mandatory for government contracts

================================================================================
DOCUMENT STRUCTURE
================================================================================

The PWD SoR 2022 (Revised) contains comprehensive chapters covering all aspects
of civil construction work:

CHAPTER 1: GENERAL, SITE FACILITIES & SAFETY
- Site establishment and clearance
- Temporary facilities
- Safety requirements and equipment
- General construction requirements

CHAPTER 2: EXCAVATION, FILLING & SITE DEVELOPMENT
- Earthwork excavation
- Backfilling and compaction
- Site grading and leveling
- Soil stabilization

CHAPTER 3: BRICK SOLING, CONCRETE WORKS, DPC
- Brick flat soling
- Plain cement concrete (PCC)
- Damp proof course (DPC)
- Foundation works

CHAPTER 4: BRICK WORKS AND RELATED CONSTRUCTION
- Brick masonry in various mortars
- Block masonry
- Stone masonry
- Plastering and rendering

ADDITIONAL CHAPTERS:
The schedule includes detailed chapters on all major construction activities
including finishing works, roofing, waterproofing, painting, and other
specialized construction works.

KEY FEATURES
================================================================================

ZERO LEAD RATES:
- Rates provided are on "zero lead" basis
- Excludes transportation/carriage costs
- Local adjustments required based on project location

COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE:
- Material specifications
- Labor requirements
- Construction methodologies
- Quality control measures
- Measurement and payment procedures

STANDARDIZATION:
- Uniform rates across government projects
- Consistent quality standards
- Standardized measurement methods
- Clear specifications for materials and workmanship
