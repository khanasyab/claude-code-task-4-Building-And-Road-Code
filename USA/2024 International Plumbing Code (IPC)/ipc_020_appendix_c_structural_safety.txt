Appendix B Rates of Rainfall For Various Cities
This appendix is informative and is not part of the code.
User notes:
About this appendix: The design of storm water drainage systems (Chapter 11) requires determination of the design rainfall event for the geographic location of the building. Appendix B provides a list of major cities in the United States along with the 1-hour duration, 100-year occurrence rainfall rate.
