Chapter 9 Permanent Residential Spas and Permanent Residential Exercise Spas
User notes:
About this chapter: Chapter 9 regulates permanent residential spas and exercise spas by reference to Chapter 5 and exempting certain sections of that chapter as those regulations are not needed for a residential setting.
Section 901 General
CDP
901.1 Scope.
CDP
This chapter shall govern the design, installation, construction and repair of permanently installed residential spas and exercise spas intended for residential use.
901.2 General.
CDP
Permanent residential spas and permanent residential exercise spas shall comply with Chapter 5 except that Sections 504.1, 504.1.1 and
508.1 shall not apply. Such spas shall comply with the requirements of Chapter 3.
Section 902 Safety features
902.1 Instructions and safety signage.
Instructions and safety signage shall comply with the manufacturer’s recommendations and the requirements of the local jurisdiction.
